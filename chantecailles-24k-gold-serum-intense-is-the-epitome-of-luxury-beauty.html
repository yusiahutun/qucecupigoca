Chantecailles 24K Gold Serum Intense Is the Epitome of Luxury

May 20, 2024
blog

With beauty formulations, it can often feel like you have to choose between innovative, science-backed ingredients and a luxurious product experience. Over my decade-plus years as a beauty editor, many products that fall into either category—but not both—have crossed my desk. Chantecaille is a rare brand that excels at striking a perfect balance between the two. It was among the first luxury beauty brands to infuse makeup with skincare, use stem cells and botanicals in its formulas, and give back to environmental conservation efforts. And it's now been bringing the very best of nature and science to the prestige beauty space for 25 years.

Instead of traditional silver, the brand is commemorating the occasion with something even better: gold. Chantecaille recently launched 24K Gold Serum Intense, an icon-in-the-making and the newest addition to the high-performance Gold Collection. With unique, results-driven ingredients, thoughtful design, and ultra-chic packaging, the smoothing, radiance-boosting serum exemplifies everything devotees, myself included, love about the brand.

Chantecaille 24K Gold Serum Intense

Chantecaille 24K Gold Serum Intense

As the name indicates, gold is at the center of this formula, and not just in its appearance—the star ingredient is a gold vitamin C complex. Tests have shown that when bound to vitamin C, gold helps stabilize the antioxidant (which can otherwise easily break down from exposure to light and air, among other factors). As someone who's tried countless vitamin C formulas, it's not easy to impress me, but this particular benefit completely sold me. Lab results for this ingredient have shown to improve skin brightness by 18 percent within one month of use.

The precious metal creates another power cocktail for this serum though its pairing with pentapeptide. Ingredient lab tests showed that gold enhances the latter's effectiveness, with this particular combination helping to reduce wrinkle depth and size more efficiently.

Other ingredient highlights include Next Generation HA, a proprietary, fast-acting form of hyaluronic acid that was found to help improve the appearance of wrinkles in a matter of hours, as well as firming and radiance-improving enzymes and line-minimizing botanicals.

Chantecaille

The serum landed on my doorstep at the perfect time. I'm pregnant and as a result of hormonal changes, have been dealing with uneven tone, as well as dryness and sensitivity. To manage these concerns, I've been on the hunt for a new vitamin C serum with cutting-edge active ingredients that also feels like a treat to use (always check with your doctor before using any new skincare during pregnancy). Chantecaille 24K Gold Serum Intense checks all the boxes.

The formula comes in a gorgeous gold bottle featuring an innovative dropper that automatically loads as you twist open the cap. This gives you a precise single dose that's enough to cover your face and neck, but not so much that any of the precious formula is wasted, in keeping with the company's longtime ethos of sustainability.

The serum has a silky texture that is absorbed within a matter of seconds and leaves my skin feeling incredibly soft and looking glowier, plumper, and smoother than it's been in recent memory. In the morning, I apply a dropper's worth to my face and neck after cleansing. As someone with combination skin, I appreciate that it doesn't leave any sort of residue or sheen—just a deliciously velvety feel. I follow with Chantecaille Gold Energizing Eye Serum and 24 Karat Gold Firming Moisturizer, both similarly imbued with gold for leveled-up, line-softening effects. By the time I apply my sunscreen, my skin already appears calmer and more radiant—proof that it's getting the care it deserves.

The visible results and premium product details make me feel like I'm investing in myself and my skin. And if that's not luxury you can trust, I'm not sure what is.

Deanna Pai is a freelance beauty writer and editor currently based in New York. She has more than 10 years of experience writing about beauty and wellness.
