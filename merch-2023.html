Taste of Country's New Merch Is Here, in Time for the Holidays

blog

Taste of Country is making your holiday shopping a lot easier this year with a new line of merchandise in our store. Country music fans can now get their hands on unique T-shirts and sweatshirts for everyone on their list, including themselves!

Artist Campaign Shirts (Buy Here!)

Does your dream election ballot include your favorite country stars? Well, now you can campaign all you want for some of your favorites. Choose either "Dolly / Reba '24" or "Wallen / Hardy '24." Each design comes in assorted color combinations. Fans can opt for a hooded sweatshirt option in both designs, too.

Put Your Damn Phone Away Shirt (Buy Here!)

There are a few things that can really ruin your country concert experience: A bad seat, drunk people ... and phones. You know the ones who are essentially live-streaming the entire event? With the "Put Your Damn Phone Away" T-shirt, you can rest assured your message will be received without having to say a word. Choose a T-shirt or sweatshirt option in two different color combinations.

Yellowstone Train Station Tour Shirt (Buy Here!)

While most Yellowstone train tours sound lovely, this is one train station you don't want to end up at. Fans of the show Yellowstone will respect the joke and adore this Train Station Tours shirt. Choose from three different colors for the Dutton Ranch lover in your life.

All T-shirts are unisex fit in sizes small to 3XL, while the sweatshirts are classic fleece pullovers, also available in sizes small to 3XL.

Shop at TasteofCountryStore.com and stay up-to-date with new designs and products under the "New Arrivals" tab.
