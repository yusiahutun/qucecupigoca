Why Kaia Gerber Didn't Attend the 2023 Oscars with Austin Butler

April 04, 2024
blog

Kaia Gerber and Austin Butler did not make their much-awaited Oscars debut tonight as many fans expected; instead, the Elvis heartthrob walked the champagne carpet solo.

Butler looked dapper as ever in a sleek black velvet suit by Saint Laurent. He paired the look with a classic white button-down, a black bow tie, black shiny dress shoes, and Cartier jewelry.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

He did not bring a date to the ceremony—which was held at the Dolby Theatre in Los Angeles—as his model girlfriend remains across the country at South by Southwest, a media and entertainment event that takes place annually in Austin, Texas. (At least she had Austin in her heart.)

The festival runs from March 10 to 19, and Gerber has been participating in events throughout the weekend. On her Instagram Story today, she shared photos and videos of herself in a chic, sleeveless white dress by The Row at the premiere of her upcoming comedy film, Bottoms, which took place yesterday.

So that explains why she couldn't make it to the carpet all the way in Los Angeles to accompany her man tonight.

Frazer Harrison//Getty Images

The model and the actor have been dating since December 2021. Back in May 2022, they made their Cannes Film Festival debut—one of their first public appearances—to celebrate the premiere of Elvis, the Baz Luhrmann film in which Butler portrays the King of Rock 'n' Roll, Elvis Presley. Butler is nominated for Best Actor at tonight's Oscars for his role in the biopic.
