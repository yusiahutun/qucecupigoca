1. EJ, 31

2. Andre, 42

In 1981, Magic and his ex-girlfriend Melissa Mitchell had a baby boy together. According to Cinemaholic, the couple split shortly after having Andre. Mitchell raised Andre in Michigan, but he did spend time with Magic’s side of the family during summer holidays.

Andre has been with his wife Lisa for 10 years and they have two children together.

She was just three days old when Magic and Cookie adopted her on December 23, 1994. Elisa has been outspoken about her adoption, explaining that she never felt “out of place” in the Johnson household. According to Adoptions with Love, she still has a relationship with her biological family. “Thinking about how I have the Johnson family and I also have my birth family, I just feel like I definitely will never be alone,” she stated.
