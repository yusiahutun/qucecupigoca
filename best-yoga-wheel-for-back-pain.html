You don’t have to be a yogi to know there are bountiful health benefits to yoga exercise. It can help you manage your blood sugar, improve your mental health, soothe fibromyalgia pain, and even help you lose weight.

Yoga is especially great for stretching muscles and relieving nerve pressure that may result in back pain. The best part is that you don’t have to be an expert to reap these benefits, especially with the help of a yoga wheel. The round, padded tool stretches just about every muscle, and can be used as a prop during a formal yoga class, or to stretch your back while watching TV during a relaxing night at home.

What is a yoga wheel?

Unlike foam rollers which are wider and shorter, yoga wheels are firmer and more effective in providing pain relief to targeted areas. They’re easy to use, too, even for those of us who lack flexibility. Simply press your back against the wheel and allow your muscles to stretch. You can then roll the wheel back and forth along your spine to decompress vertebrae and relax muscles.

They range in size from 6-12 inches, so you can store them anywhere. You also won’y have to worry about their durability — most have a weight capacity of 500-1,000 pounds and are strong enough to handle anything.
