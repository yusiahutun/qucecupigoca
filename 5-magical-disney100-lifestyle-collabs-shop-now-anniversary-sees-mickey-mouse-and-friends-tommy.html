5 magical Disney100 lifestyle collabs to shop now: the anniversary sees Mickey Mouse and friends on

September 17, 2024
blog
Disney’s tie-up with Tommy Hilfiger for its 100th anniversary saw the release of a preppy chic capsule wardrobe that pays tribute to the original Mickey cartoon character. The collection is nothing short of a celebration of Walt Disney’s legacy and features a variety of gender-inclusive pieces: a relaxed fit jumper boasting eye-catching Mickey artwork that is larger than life, a commemorative jacket, and rugby shirts with Mickey Mouse and Friends character patches stitched onto them.

Mickey accents are playfully scattered across striped, flared skirts and button downs, with Tommy Hilfiger’s signature America-inspired red, white and blue colours in all pieces.

2. Disney100 x Charlotte Tilbury

For make-up and magic lovers, Charlotte Tilbury has great news for you: the beauty brand has teamed up with Disney to release limited editions of its bestselling products. The Charlotte Tilbury Beauty Wands now come in new Disney100 edition colours, including Spotlight, Pink and Gold, embellished with a Tinkerbell design, while the brand’s award-winning Magic Cream features a new limited-edition bejewelled lid adorned with a Tinkerbell silhouette as well.

In a promotional animation produced by Disney, we see Charlotte Tilbury herself transform into a Disney character, appearing alongside Tinkerbell in the enchanting short.

To further spread the magic, Charlotte Tilbury will be hosting limited free, first-come-first-serve live virtual masterclasses this month to teach looks inspired by the likes of Disney’s Mulan, Elsa, Alice in Wonderland and more.

3. Disney x Karl Lagerfeld

The limited-edition Disney x Karl Lagerfeld capsule collection features a rare sketch of Donald Duck by Karl Lagerfeld himself. Created in 2004 as a personal homage from Lagerfeld to his favourite Disney character, the sketch celebrated Donald’s 70th anniversary, reimagining the cartoon duck in Lagerfeld’s distinctive figure.

The collection imagines Disney in Karl’s Paris with vibrant colours evoking the brand’s playful sophistication as well as Disney’s spirit. Pieces feature Lagerfeld’s Donald Duck sketch, complete with both the Disney logo alongside the iconic Karl house monogram. From everyday statement pieces to accessories and bags, the collection is a fun way to add colour to your wardrobe.

4. Adidas Mickey and Minnie Sambas for Disney’s 100th Anniversary

Sneaker giant Adidas joins in with the Disney100 celebrations with an all-new rendition of the classic vegan Sambas featuring Disney’s Mickey and Minnie Mouse. Each shoe sports a Mickey and Minnie hand, forming a cheeky heart when the left and right feet are beside each other.

The Sambas have kept their classic low-top silhouette with Adidas’ signature three-stripe logo on the sides, atop a flat rubber sole. The vegan construction comes in an effort to help end plastic waste.

5. Pandora x Disney 100 Limited Edition Collection

Pandora is revealing nine limited-edition dangle charms throughout the year to celebrate Disney’s 100 years of wonder, with six of them already available to purchase. The charms are part of a collection that goes on the Disney 100th Anniversary Moments Snake Chain bracelet. The brand’s latest release centres on Simba from The Lion King, an adorable charm with the cartoon lion resting his paw on a lab-created diamond.

Previous releases are inspired by Minnie Mouse, Donald Duck and even Cinderella’s Enchanted Carriage, all featuring a lab-created diamond and a “Disney100” engraving, with every purchase coming with an exclusive collector’s box.
