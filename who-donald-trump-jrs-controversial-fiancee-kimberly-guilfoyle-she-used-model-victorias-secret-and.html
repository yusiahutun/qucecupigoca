What nationality is Kimberly Guilfoyle?

Guilfoyle was born in 1969 in San Francisco. She was raised by her Irish immigrant father after her Puerto Rico-born mother died while she was still young. She studied law at the University of San Francisco.

She is a former model

Guilfoyle modelled for Victoria’s Secret and Macy’s before she worked as a prosecutor in California.

She worked at Fox – and was depicted in the film Bombshell

In 2004, she got a Court TV hosting gig which led to shows on Fox. Her time at the conservative station was depicted in Oscar-winning movie Bombshell, which centres around former chairman Roger Ailes’ sexual misconduct at the network. The film features a Guilfoyle lookalike showcasing her outspoken support for Ailes – much like Guilfoyle did in real life.

She left Fox in 2018 amid sexual harassment allegations against her, per Huffington Post, and later started working as a senior adviser for then president Donald Trump.

Who is Kimberly Guilfoyle’s ex-husband?

Guilfoyle was married to California governor Gavin Newsom from 2001 to 2006 per Business Insider, with the pair even dubbed the “new Kennedys” at the time. She then married Eric Villency – heir to his family’s interior design firm – in 2006, and they had a son named Ronan Anthony later that year. They divorced in 2009.

She began dating Donald Trump Jr in 2018, but Guilfoyle had reportedly known the Trump family for several years prior, while Trump Jr was still married to his first wife, Vanessa Trump.
