Trans-Siberian Orchestra Announce 2019 Holiday Tour

March 03, 2024
blog

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) – Trans-Siberian Orchestra has today (Aug. 19) announced the dates for their 2019 Christmas and Other Stories tour.

The annual 66-date holiday trek is scheduled to kick off November 13 in Green Bay, Wisconsin and will make stops in Portland, San Jose, Nashville, Jacksonville and Houston before wrapping in Chicago on December 30.

The tour, which promises all-new staging, production and effects, will offer exclusive access to the best tickets with its “12 Days of TSO Verified Fan Registration” on Monday here. Presale will begin for all TSO fan club members on  September 4 for November 13-30 tour dates and on September 5 for December 1- 30 tour dates. Those who are not fan club members but have signed up for “12 Days” will be able to access pre-sales on September 9 and 10. General public on sale September 13 here.
