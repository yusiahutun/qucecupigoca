Get ready to get lucky! St. Patrick's Day is coming up on March 17, so you best wear green and channel all things Irish and celebratory. This holiday of merriment is best celebrated with some humor too, so here we give you 100 funny St. Patrick's Day jokes for the occasion.

As you gather 'round with all your friends in green, pull out these clean one-liners about leprechauns, shamrocks, rainbows, and all things Irish to get your laugh on for the party too.

Looking for some more funnies for the occasion?
