Nature Photographers to Follow For Inspiration

August 28, 2024
blog

We live in an incredibly beautiful world. When we go out, it is impossible not to admire the beauty of nature. People take their photos to immortalize this fascinating landscape they encounter. But the world is so big that one may not have the chance to see all parts of the world. In this case, the importance of nature photography shows itself. 

In this article, we have compiled the 10 most incredible nature photographers to follow for inspiration for you. These are the nature photographers whose accounts I think can be both life filled and inspired;

Michael Shainblum

Michael Shainblum is a well-known American photographer who specializes in producing  atmospheric and dramatic photographs. He has a decade of experience in professional photography and filmmaking. Michael’s success has led to him receiving commissions from prestigious multinational clients including BMW, Google, Disney, Samsung, and Nike. Michael’s work has also been widely disseminated by well-known media organizations including National Geographic, The Weather Channel, Huffington Post and Wired Magazine.

Sarah Lyndsay

Sarah is a self potrait, landscape and NFT photographer based in Canada. She gives education and workshops to help people learn how to use their cameras. She is widely known for her signature yellow dress that is often seen in her self-portraits. It is always a pleasure to see her work.

Killian Schönberger

Killian is a German photographer who specializes forests, mountains and especially foggy scenes. He create his own perspective after realize that he is colourblind. When getting the photograph of a forest scene, he couldn’t distinguish the different green and brown tones. But, he did not care about these tones, just look at the patterns.

Marc Adamus

Marc Adamus is a landscape photographer famous for his long photographic trips often take long weeks or months. He’s based in Western North America, but he spent his lots of time capturing beautiful landscapes all over the world. He is adventurer and he found countless undiscovered locations.

Daniel Kordan

Daniel grew up in the beautiful lake region near Moscow. His interest in photography began at an early age, as he was born and raised in this unique landscape. He completed an art painting school as a kid. But then he moved away from art and became a quantum physicist. Photography, which he continued as a hobby, became his profession after a while. He currently has 1.8 million followers on Instagram and gives photo-workshops around the world.

Senna Relax

Senna Relax, a photographer based in Switzerland, takes us to the places we want to be with the videos he shares as well as the photos he takes. When you look at his account, sometimes you admire the views you see on a train journey, and sometimes you witness a pleasant breakfast view in the Maldives. The impressive nature videos he shares are one of the features that make Senna unique.

Erin Babnik

Erin actually studied art history and needed to take photos for her research. When she came out for this, she was fascinated by nature photography and decided to deepen her interest. Now she takes amazing photos. Dressing in shades of purple every time she went to take pictures became her characteristic feature.

İlhan Eroğlu

Eroğlu, whose biggest passion was painting since his childhood, was working intensively in the textile sector. After realizing that he enjoyed taking photos during his travels to Europe, he found himself traveling around the country to take photos and started to organize photography tours. You should take a look at his gallery to see unique photos from many parts of the world.

Demas Rusli

Demas is a Sydney-based photographer and designer who pushes the boundaries of photography with unique perspectives and creative editing techniques. He specializes in urban, landscape, architecture and aerial photography. Demas’s gallery is very unusual and colorful with the photographs he shared with different perspectives.

Stian Klo

Stian is a very successful Norwegian photographer. When he got his first camera, he tried various kind of photography, street, macro, wildlife, long exposures and outdoor photography. Known as a mountain photographer, Stian is particularly successful in taking Arctic photographs and has taken impressive photographs.

When you follow a wonderful nature photographer, you’ll not only see the unique landscapes of the world, but you’ll also have the chance to improve your photography skills. These are bright individuals with amazing talents.
