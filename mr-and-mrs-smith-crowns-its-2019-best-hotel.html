Mr and Mrs Smith crowns its 2019 best hotel

September 26, 2024
blog

Casa Fortunato in Lisbon has been crowned Mr and Mrs Smith's 2019 Best Hotel at its annual awards. The former townhouse has been transformed into a nine-suite hotel, situated in the elegant neighbourhood of Amoreiras.

The Art Deco property was praised for its "stylish statements, exceptional service and next-generation guesthouse approach". Each suite has a unique appeal, although there is a commonality of modern and classic design throughout, as well as a focus on eclecticism which offers a homely feel.

The space designed by owners and architects António Falcão Costa Lopes and Filipa Fortunato, who also live in the building.

"Every year we’re bowled over by amazing stays with incredible stories but this year’s winners list feels particularly strong," said Mr and Mrs Smith founder James Lohan. "And in the overall winner we’ve got a hotel that embodies the very concept of ‘boutique’ – a unique, family-run, nine-suite townhouse in the city of the moment which we hope even more people will now discover and enjoy."

Other winners included Palazzo Daniele in Puglia, Italy, which was named the Best-Dressed Hotel, while Best Budget Boutique went to Sands Hotel & Spa in Palm Springs.
Salt of Palmar in Mauritius was crowned Best Local Hero (which we visited and reviewed earlier this year) and Best Smith Villa was won by Uxua Alma Casa Anderson in Bahia, Brazil. Euphoria Mystras, Greece, topped the Most Spoiling Spa category.
