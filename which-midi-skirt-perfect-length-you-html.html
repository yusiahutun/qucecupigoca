Which midi skirt is the perfect length for YOU?

August 26, 2024
blog

There’s only one skirt to be seen in this summer — the midi. It’s chic and oh so versatile.

Throw yours over swimwear at the beach, team it with a floaty blouse for the office, or dress it up with a silky cami in the evening.

You may have to go to some lengths, however, to find a midi of the perfect, well... length.

Dinah van Tulleken shares a guide to selecting flattering midi skirts this summer, pictured: SHORTEST 27 INCHES (from waistband to hem) £29, french connection.com

It should sit comfortably on the waist. Too long and you drown in fabric, too short and you end up with a frumpy, just-below-the-knee look. Getting it right is crucial, but there are many lengths around — all the skirts here are sold as ‘midis’, but they vary by ten inches.
