Anerlisa Muigai Rebukes Millicent Omanga, Robert Alai Over Attacks on Her Mum Tabitha: &quot;Stop Lies&quot;

February 07, 2024
blog
  • Anerlisa Muigai has joined the mix involving her mum, Senator Tabitha Karanja Keroche, and her critics who want her out
  • Millicent Omanga and Kileleshwa MCA are among the leaders who criticised Karanja's move to call for the ousting of Nakuru Governor, Susan Kihika
  • However, Anerlisa harshly told them to leave her mum alone and let her work for the people who voted for her

Keroche heiress Anerlisa Muigai has strongly come out to defend her mother, Nakuru Senator Tabitha Karanja, amid calls to oust her.

It all started when Karanja strongly condemned the attack on Nakuru War Memorial Hospital by the county government.

She said Nakuru residents will never forgive Governor Susan Kihika over her role in the siege of the hospital.

Anerrisa Muigai goes after mum's critics

Karanja urged Members of the County Assembly (MCAs) to table an impeachment motion against Kihika.

What followed was renowned politician Millicent Omanga calling for Karanja to be removed as the senator while calling for accountability in the county.

Omanga accused Senator Karanja of witch-hunting after asking MCAs to oust Kihika and said Kihika should be supported to deliver on her mandate to the people of Nakuru.

In the same breath, Kileleshwa MCA Robert Alai also took to his X page to criticise Karanja's leadership and advise her to address her county's governor when not intoxicated.

Alai wrote:

"The problem with Tabitha Karanja is that she spends more time drinking alcohol than even trying to understand what's happening in Nakuru County."

However, Anerlisa did not waste time trying to defend her mum. She took to her Instastory on Instagram to warn the two politicians to keep her mother's name out of their mouths.

While addressing Alai, the 35-year-old wrote:

"Robert and I repeat, Robert. Stop telling lies about my mum."

She had previously told off Omanga, saying:

"Don't try on my mum. When she is given a job, she does it at her best. She was given a job by her Nakuru County residence, and she will do it at her best."

Anerlisa accompanies mum to swearing-in

Elsewhere, the heiress, who is no doubt close to her mum, once shared a video depicting her escorting her mother into the parliament.

The Keroche heiress was proud to accompany her mum for her swearing-in as the Senator of Nakuru county.

In her caption, the beauty asked God to bless her mother; she also noted that she was confident about her mum's leadership skills.
