Who Is Lovely Mimi?

The sensation of Instagram was born as Myha Thi Luong on August 20, 1990, in Vietnam, a country in Asia. Her parents, whose names are not known to the public, emigrated to the United States of America in 1993 and settled in Silver Spring, Maryland, and later in Virginia.

Upon arrival in the United States, Mimi began studying at a high school. Later she dropped out of school to pursue a career as a nail artist. The high school dropout now has nail studios in Atlanta and also works as a nail artist for various companies in the country. If you want to hire or support her, her nail studio is called Luong’s Lovely Nails.

In addition to her nail business, Mimi also sings. In September 2016 she played with the London-based English boyband 5ive in their hip-hop hit entitled We Don’t Care. The famous Instagram star was recently cast as a member of the VH1 show Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta.

Her Family – Husband and Children

Lovely Mimi is the wife of Remy Skinner, a handsome young man who often appears in the background of her videos on Instagram. The couple got married in 2010 after they had been together for a long time. Their marriage is blessed with three children. Their daughter is named Juice Skinner, while their son was named Jayy Skinner. Together they run an Instagram account called juicenjayy.

Remi and Mimi have a vibrant relationship with each other and you can often see them spending their moments happily with their children. Skinner is also on Instagram, but not as popular as his wife. He is known on Instagram as Remy The Boss. The Skinners currently live in Atlanta, Georgia.

Where Is She Now?
