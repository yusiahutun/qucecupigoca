Rudy Youngblood arrested for being drunk and disorderly

An actor best known for starring in Apocalypto was arrested for being drunk and disorderly in a casino.

Rudy Youngblood, 34, was allegedly verbally abusive to other poker players at Miccosukee Resort & Gaming in Miami.

Police called to the casino by security staff said the actor reeked of alcohol and was slurring, TMZ reported.

Arrested: Actor Rudy Youngblood, 34, (pictured in 2015) was arrested for being drunk and disorderly in a casino

He was arrested in January and charged with disorderly intoxication, but the charges were later dropped, according to sources.
