1. Ed Sheeran - $200 million

Ed Sheeran is among the highest-paid rappers globally, with an estimated net worth of $200 million. Most of his income has come from tours, with the Divide Tour grossing over $775 million between March 2017 and August 2019.

He also makes money from endorsements, especially from Heinz that struck a deal with him since he has a ketchup logo tattooed on his arm. Some of his best rap songs include All About It, You Need Me, I Don't Need You, Eraser, Take It Back, and Sing.

2. Stormzy - $25 million

Stormzy is among British rappers with the highest net worth of $25 million. The king of grime makes his money from tours, but that has reduced significantly since Covid-19. He also has collaborations with many brands, including Adidas. But the musician also receives his money from festivals, especially the Merky Festival in Ibiza, which he owns.
