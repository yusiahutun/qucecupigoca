Aanchal Kumar Age, Career, Personal Life- Biography Info

Aanchal Kumar is an Indian Model and Actress (born 24 October 1979). For her cameo roles in Bluffmaster and Fashion, she is well known. She was a 2010 Bigg Boss 4 contestant.

Early life

She originates from Chandigarh. She completed her studies at St. Stephen’s, Chandigarh.

Personal life

Aanchal announced her wedding to her long-time boyfriend Anupam Mittal in June 2013 and got married at Jaipur Palace on 4 July 2013.

Career

In 1999, Aanchal Kumar won the Mega Model Contest at Gladrags. For brands like Rexona, Sunsilk, and Bombay Dyeing, she has also done commercial assignments.

She has also made a few music videos, including ‘Aaina‘ by Jagjit Singh, ‘Mukhra Tumahra’ by Hans Raj Hans, and ‘Dupatta Tera Satrang Da’ by Surjit Bindrakhia. Aanchal has appeared in a few Bollywood movies, including Fashion and Bluffmaster!.

In the Indian version of the popular British show, The Big Brother, Aanchal appeared in Bigg Boss (season 4). In the sixth week, she was voted out.

Facts

In 1999, Aanchal Kumar bagged the trophy of the ‘Gladrags Mega Model Contest’.

For celebrity designers such as Manish Malhotra, Ritu Kumar, etc, she has done many ramp shows. For many years, she has been a part of Lakme Fashion Week as well.

She has done numerous TV advertisements, print advertisements such as Sunsilk, Rexona, and Bombay Dyeing, etc. In the movies ‘Bluffmaster’ (2005) and ‘Fashion’ (2008), she made a cameo and also appeared in a few music videos.
