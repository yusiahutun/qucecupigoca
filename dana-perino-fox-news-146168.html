Dana Perino, Ainsley Earnhardt Fox News On-Air Personalities

Regardless if you're a loyal Fox News watcher or just a casual one, you've probably noticed all the blonde on-air personalities, from Dana Perino to Ainsley Earhardt. However, with so many anchors coming and going, it can be a little confusing figuring out who's who. And despite their physical similarities, there's a lot more to the "blonde pundits" than simply being, well, "blonde pundits."

For Dana, she was a former White House press secretary under President George W. Bush. After leaving the White House, she started contributing regularly to Fox News, including on shows like The Five, and in 2017, she was given her own show, The Daily Briefing with Dana Perino. She's been called a "blonde firecracker" and even found time to publish a New York Times bestselling book, And the Good News Is…: Lessons and Advice from the Bright Side.

Ainsley is also another popular on-air personality. The 41-year-old mom co-hosts the morning show, Fox & Friends. She recently made headlines when she wore pants on the show, which you think wouldn't be a big deal. However, according to former co-hosts like Gretchen Carlson, ex-CEO Roger Ailes didn't like his female anchors to wear pants and strongly encouraged them to wear skirts! Ainsley's act of defiance showed she was "sticking it to the man," so to speak.

In addition to wearing pantsuits, Ainsley is also an author and recently published the book Through Your Eyes: My Child's Gift to Me, which chronicled her path to motherhood and other sweet inspirational anecdotes. The book is a follow-up to her 2016 picture book, Take Heart, My Child: A Mother's Dream. She also got in some hot water in 2017 for her comments about the tragic Sutherland Springs church shooting.
