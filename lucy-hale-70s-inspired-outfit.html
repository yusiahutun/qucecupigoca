Lucy Hale's '70s-Inspired Outfit

March 07, 2024
blog

We’d be pretty little liars if we said we didn’t love Lucy Hale’s latest ensemble.

On Friday, the 27-year-old Liars star did what most of us do on a weekday morning and grabbed a fresh cup of iced cold coffee at her local Starbucks in Los Angeles. Sure, Hale may have needed her A.M. dose of caffeine before officially kicking off her day, but the beauty’s casual daytime outfit proves she definitely had a particular pep in her step.

Channeling all things ‘70s, the brunette rocked a pair of high-waist bell-bottom crop flares (does it get anymore ‘70s than that?) with a red-orange printed cropped tank and a black leather chain strap handbag. She styled the pieces with oversize rounded sunglasses, modern silver loafers, and an off-duty tied-up ‘do.

So how much does the actress love her fashion? The star took to Instagram yesterday to share a glowing selfie of herself rocking the same tortoise-covered shades.

She also posted a too-cute video playing with her adorable pup, Elvis, as she once more wore the accessory.

Sometimes you can’t help but wear something over and over again.
