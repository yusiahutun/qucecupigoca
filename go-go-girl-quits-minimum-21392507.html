Mary added: “Before Instagram I worked in a restaurant, basically 9-5, and I earned $10 an hour (around £7.50 an hour).

“I hated the job. I worked for a boss I did not agree with, and with people who most didn't put effort into their job…

“I was stressed and worked all the time, sometimes six to seven days a week, and only made enough money to cover bills.

“I missed time with family and always had to work on weekends and holidays and I dealt with eating and sleeping issues because of the long hours."

As the dancer failed to find happiness in her restaurant job, she began thinking of other ways to earn money.

Mary realised she could start posting dancing clips online – and planned to grow her go-go girl fan club further.

She explained: "I decided to pursue Instagram and social media influencing because I had been told my whole life that I could model because of my natural curvy fit figure.

"I took my personality and fan base I grew from the go-go stage and applied it to my personality in front of the camera.

“I figured since I had ‘fans’ from being in the nightclub scene that I should start up with social media.”

The Instagram bombshell’s career soon took off – thanks to a photoshoot with well-known photographer @thisiseames.

Mary recalled: “I had my first real photoshoot in September 2017 with the photographer @thisiseames.

“At the start of our shoot I had 835 followers.

“By the end of the day of shooting from him posting me on his story of our behind the scenes footage, I gained 1000 followers in a matter of hours…

“As soon as @thisiseames and I started posting the photos from our shoot, they immediately became viral and within 1.5 months I hit 100,000 (followers).

“After that I grew 100,000 for each of the next few months.”

As Mary boasts a fanbase of 1.5 million nowadays, she’s able to make a fortune from Instagram.

In a year, she makes around £85,000 in brand deals and sponsorships.

The influencer said: “After two years of my social media growing, I not only have most of my free time back, I now make in one month what I would use to take me a year, by simply posting sexy photos and twerk videos.”

While Mary has celebrated lots of success in her career, she has to hide her work from her dad.

The bombshell, who has an associate's degree in business and finance, doesn’t think her father would approve of her glamorous antics.
