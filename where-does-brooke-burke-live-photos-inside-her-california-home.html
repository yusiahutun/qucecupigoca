Where Does Brooke Burke Live? Photos Inside Her California Home

August 31, 2024
blog

If only we could all live like Brooke Burke! The former Dancing With the Stars host lives in an enormous house that’s big enough for her and her four kids, Shaya, Neriah, Heaven and Sierra, in Malibu, California.

The doting mom used to live with her ex-husband David Charvet, but after seven years of marriage, they decided to get a divorce. However, this wasn’t the first time Brooke split from her hubby. In 2005, she left her first husband, Garth Fisher, after they were married for four years. But now that that’s over with, Brooke is just enjoying her California home with the kids and her new boyfriend, Scott Rigsby.

Once you walk inside Brooke’s humble abode, you’ll notice she has a lot of space in her living room. The fitness guru does workouts there for her app, Brooke Burke Body, and sometimes she might even invite a friend over to exercise.

“BFF Burn,” the mom of four said on Instagram next to a clip of her and her pal working out in front of the couch. If Brooke wants to prepare a healthy meal for her family, then she knows where to go. The TV personality has a beautiful garden in her backyard that’s chock full of yummy vegetables. She showed it off to her fans on July 8 when Brooke posted a photo of her gardening. “Go green,” the post was captioned.

But Brooke’s favorite part of the house has to be the driveway, and that’s only because she takes a ton of selfies in her car. When Sierra graduated from school, the Need for Speed: Underground star couldn’t wait for everyone to get to the “drive-in” before she snapped a pic of her kids huddled in the vehicle.

“Grad gang‼️” she gushed online. “Who could’ve ever imagined that the #classof2020 would go down in history. Here we go, road trip to the drive-in.” Brooke’s house must be every parents’ dream.
