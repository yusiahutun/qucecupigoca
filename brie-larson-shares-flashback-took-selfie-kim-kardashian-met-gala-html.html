Brie Larson shares a flashback to when she took a selfie with Kim Kardashian at the Met Gala

August 07, 2024
blog

The first Monday in May should have seen some of the leading tastemakers celebrating the connection between fashion and time at the Met Gala.

Instead, former guest Brie Larson had to satisfy herself with an awkwardly timed throwback selfie from her appearance at the 2017 edition.

The 30-year-old Captain Marvel star reposted a selfie taken after she stopped by the bathroom and got swept up in a Kim Kardashian–led mass mirror selfie. 

Surprise entrance: Brie Larson, 30, commemorated the postponed Met Gala on Monday with a throwback post of when she got stuck in a Kardashian–Jenner clan selfie at the 2017 edition

Brie was a last-minute interloper in the photo, which was snapped by Kylie Jenner, so she looked comically cramped off the the side and just above model Slick Woods' shoulder.

'Seems like we're having a #MetBall throwback moment today so I'd to take this time to remind everyone of the year I was trying to wash my hands in the bathroom and ended up in a Kardashian selfie,' she wrote in the caption.

The Kardashian–Jenner clan definitely seemed to be the instigators, though selfies are technically banned at the Met Gala.

 Kylie and Kim made up the front line, while Kendall Jenner stood just behind them with rapper A$AP Rocky and her model friend Luka Sabbat.

Star studded: She posted humorous blow-ups of the photo, which featured Kim Kardashian, Kylie and Kendall Jenner, Paris Jackson, P. Diddy, Frank Ocean and Luka Sabbat, among other

Paris Jackson hovered above the crowd, while P. Diddy and Frank Ocean beamed to the left of Brie.

She joined other celebrities commiserating about the postponed Met Gala on social media, including Kylie, who shared her stunning outfits going back to her first red carpet appearance at the event in 2016. 

Her dress wasn't visible at all in the selfie, but she stunned in a plunging Chanel Haute Couture dress with a tiered gold skirt and a leafy silver and gold top.

She spiced up the look by sweeping her short blonde tresses to one side and decorating her hair with gold leaf. 

Golden goddess: Her dress wasn't visible at all in the selfie, but she stunned in a plunging Chanel Haute Couture dress with a tiered gold skirt and a leafy silver and gold top; shown in 2017

On the way up: Brie's Met Gala appearance came a year after she won the Oscar for Best Actress and two years before she starred as Captain Marvel in the billion-dollar grossing film

Brie's infamous selfie came at a turning point in her career after she'd received bounteous critical acclaim for her roles in Short Term 12 (2013) and Room (2015), the latter of which earned her the Academy Award for Best Actress.

Buoyed by her new status, she took on an ensemble role in the rebooted Kong: Skull Island (2017), directed her first film, Unicorn Store (2017), and joined the Marvel Cinematic Universe as Captain Marvel, whose eponymous 2019 film earned more than $1.2 billion at the box office.

She'd go on to have a small but pivotal role in Avengers: Endgame, and she's reportedly set to lead a revamped Avengers force when the remaining superheroes regroup.

'Literally me and my relationship with the Internet': Last Thursday, Brie performed her cover of Noah Cyrus' 2019 song July on the electric guitar while quarantining at home

On Thursday, Brie broke up her quarantine doldrums by performing a cover of Noah Cyrus' 2019 song July on her ice blue electric guitar.

'Literally me and my relationship with the Internet. Thank you @noahcyrus for this beautiful heartfelt song,' she captioned it.

The Oscar winner has been spending her quarantine time with her boyfriend Elijah Allan-Blitz, whom she made her red carpet debut with at the Academy Awards in February.

She previously split from her fiancé — former Phantom Planet frontman Alex Greenwald — back in January 2019.
