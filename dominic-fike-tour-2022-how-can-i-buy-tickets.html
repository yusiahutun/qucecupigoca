Dominic Fike tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

August 18, 2024
blog

SINGER/SONGWRITER Dominic Pike announced he will be going on his OUT of Order tour this fall.

This is his first tour since the release of his 2020 album What Could Possibly Go Wrong.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

When is Dominic Fike's tour?

Dominic Fike's Out of Order tour will kick off on November 6, 2022, in Seattle, Washington, and will continue for 24 shows, ending on December 6, in Tempe, Arizona.

Fike gained in popularity after releasing his 2016 EP, Don't You Forget About Me, prompting him to sign a record deal with Columbia Records.

He has collaborated with Halsey and Brockhampton and secured his first acting gig as Elliott on the popular show Euphoria.

How can I buy tickets?

Presale tickets will be available to purchase on AXS on September 29 at 10am local time.

The remaining tickets will go on sale on September 30 at 10am local time and will start at $35 per ticket.

Those looking to purchase tickets can also do so on Ticketmaster, StubHub, and SeatGeek.
