Kind Kenyan Woman Visits Ndungu Nyoro's Office, Gifts Him KSh 100k and Leaves Instantly

June 19, 2024
blog
  • Philanthropist Ndungu Nyoro took to social media to appreciate a woman who supported his education campaign
  • Nyoro shared that the woman visited his ministry's office, delivered a check worth KSh 100,000, and left
  • The news equally touched social media users as they showered the kind woman with blessings in the comment section

A kind-hearted stranger has warmed the heart of philanthropist Ndungu Nyoro following her selfless gesture to his campaign.

The woman visited Nyoro's office to deliver a package and show her support for his excellent work of educating needy kids.

The heartwarming gesture captured on CCTV footage moved Nyoro as he shared the good news online.
