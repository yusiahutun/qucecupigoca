Who Is Bella B Harris?

March 30, 2024
blog

On Thursday, Page Six reported Drake was seen at a Washington, D.C. restaurant with Bella B. Harris, an 18-year-old model. Though they arrived in separate entrances to RPM Italian on Monday night, they were still caught having a late-night dinner together.

Neither of them has commented, but rumors of the two dating had started over the summer. Here’s what you need to know about the model, who already has connections to the music industry.

She just signed a big modeling deal.

Bella told MissGuided in 2016 she was scouted when she was 13 and worked with a modeling agency in Los Angeles. When she started working, she only took on jobs after school or on weekends. The first three runways she walked were Naeem Khan, Alice + Olivia, and Sherri Hill.

On August 31, the modeling agency Elite Model Management announced they had signed her to their Empower Me Elite team. She worked at several shows during New York Fashion Week, and also shot a campaign for Ardell Beauty.

“I have so many hopes and dreams for the future. You don’t have to label or define yourself by just one thing,” she told MissGuided. “I hope to be successful at modeling and really nail all aspects of the modeling business, but I really want to do so much more.” Those goals, she said, include fashion design and interior design.

She has a connection to the music industry.

Mike Coppola//Getty Images

Bella is the daughter of legendary music producer Jimmy Jam, who is perhaps best known for producing Janet Jackson’s most famous albums alongside Terry Lewis. In fact, Janet is Bella’s godmother, according to her modeling portfolio.

Jimmy Jam, whose real name is James Samuel Harris III, congratulated Bella on Instagram when she signed with Elite. “I couldn’t be prouder of you or love you more,” he wrote. “Can’t wait to see what the future holds.” He also reposted a photo from her campaign with Ardell Beauty, writing, “I’m biased but I love this campaign featuring my beautiful daughter.”

She has a twin brother, Max, and her older brother, Tyler. Her mother, Lisa, acts as her “momager,” she told Social Lifestyle magazine.

She’s a big Rihanna fan.

Bella’s most recent Instagram, posted September 12, features her in lingerie designed by none other than Drake’s ex/possible unrequited love, Rihanna. She even tagged Rihanna’s brand, Savage X Fenty. She tagged the location as Brooklyn Navy Yard, which just so happened to be the location of Savage X Fenty’s New York Fashion Week show that same night. It’s unclear if she attended the show, went to an afterparty, or just wanted to show support.

But it is clear she looks up to Rihanna, and who doesn’t? She told MissGuided she was hired to model her Fenty-Puma line for its spring presentation. That same year, she met Rihanna behind the scenes on her tour.

“My female hero would have to be Rihanna. I was speechless when I met her and she was so nice and friendly to me,” she said. “She has accomplished and achieved so much in her career. I mean she can honestly do no wrong and I wish I could pull off all of the outfits she wears.”

In an interview with Popular TV two years before that, when Bella was just 14, she said, “Well I listen to a lot of Drake, and I love Rihanna. I was looking up her song lyrics the other day, just to make sure I was getting them right.” Given Drake's past with Rihanna, this could be an issue.

She started as a Drake fan, and then became his friend.

Before they were rumored to be together, Bella was a fan of Drake’s music. “The last song I played was ‘Fake Love’ by Drake,” she told MissGuided in the 2016 interview. And that same year, she took photos with Drake at the American Music Awards and backstage on his tour with Future. The two appear together in several photos from that year.

She gave us all a clue about Drake weeks ago.

On August 25, Bella posted sweet, couple-y Instagram photos with Drake alongside the caption, “No place I’d rather be.” If that’s not couple confirmation, what is?
