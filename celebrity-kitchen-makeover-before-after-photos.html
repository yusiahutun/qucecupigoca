6 amazing celebrity kitchen before-and-after photos to inspire you

May 05, 2024
blog

These stars have all showcased impressive home transformations

    The kitchen is the heart of any home, which is why these celebrities pulled out all the stops to ensure theirs are as stylish as they are functional, with luxurious finishes, state-of-the-art appliances and comfy dining areas to boot.

    GALLERY: 33 chic celebrity kitchens you'll love

    Frankie Bridge, Stacey Solomon and Mrs Hinch are among the famous faces who have all documented their kitchen makeovers on Instagram in recent months, sharing before-and-after photos that will inspire you to plan a renovation of your own…

    Frankie Bridge

    WATCH: Frankie Bridge shares tour of her dreamy kitchen

    Frankie Bridge and her husband Wayne have completed a full transformation of their kitchen in the past couple of years, and while it looked impeccable before, it is even better now.

    Frankie Bridge's kitchen used to be decorated cream

    While the space was once cream with an L-shaped island and breakfast bar, it is now decorated in dark grey and black tones, with a bench seating area and dining table at the centre, and pendant lights hanging overhead.

    RELATED: See more of Frankie Bridge's chic family home

    The star's kitchen has been transformed

    Stacey Solomon

    When Stacey Solomon and Joe Swash moved into their new family home, 'Pickle Cottage', the kitchen featured rustic décor with beams across the ceilings, wooden cabinets, and tiled flooring. It has since been given a complete makeover in favour of a more modern aesthetic, featuring all-white cabinets and walls, and an AGA oven with brass accents.

    Stacey Solomon's kitchen had wooden cabinets and tiled flooring

    The kitchen is now decorated white with a stone wall

    Mrs Hinch

    Mrs Hinch has been busy renovating her new family home, Hinch Farm, prior to moving in, and the kitchen is no exception. While the work is yet to be completed, Sophie has been sharing updates with her followers on social media, showing exposed brickwork, wooden cabinets and marble worktops, along with a glass-fronted door leading to the pantry.

    Mrs Hinch's kitchen was previously an empty shell

    The room has been transformed prior to the influencer moving in

    Martin and Shirlie Kemp                                                           

    Martin and Shirlie worked with Howdens to create their dream kitchen – and the results are stunning. The room has been transformed into a chic all-white kitchen with a huge island and breakfast bar with brass lights hanging overhead and wooden stools perched underneath.

    RELATED: See more of Martin and Shirlie Kemp's home makeover photos

    Martin and Shirlie Kemp's kitchen is unrecognisable

    Mollie King

    BBC Radio 1 DJ Mollie King also enlisted Howdens to overhaul her kitchen back in 2019, and proudly showcased the finished result on Instagram, saying it had been the "best experience". It features pale grey cabinets and white worktops, and was carefully designed to transform her "outdated and cramped" kitchen into a stylish and multi-functional area.

    Lucy Mecklenburgh

    Former star Lucy Mecklenburgh loves working on renovation projects, and shares all the details of her work on her dedicated Instagram account, @lucymeckinteriors. The kitchen at her family home has undergone a huge makeover since she moved in, with both the style and layout of the room transformed completely into an open-plan, modern white and grey aesthetic.
