7 plus-size spring outfit ideas 2023: Cute dresses, denim jumpsuits &amp; bomber jackets

June 20, 2024
blog

Spring is officially here, and there's no better time for a wardrobe refresh than at the start of a new season. 

Whether you're in need of some style inspiration or are on the hunt for some flattering spring staples, scroll our edit of the best plus-size spring outfit ideas to shop now, from pretty floral dresses to colourful blazers, comfortable linen trousers and more.

Plus-size spring outfit ideas

  • Plus-size belted midi dress

    A midi dress that can be dressed up or down is a must for any spring wardrobe, and River Island Plus's green shirt dress is our favourite right now. It features a stunning statement print, complete with a waist-cinching belt that's so flattering.

  • Plus-size oversized bomber jacket

    Bomber jackets are trending for spring, and H&M plus-size version is just perfect for throwing over a floaty dress or teaming jeans and trainers for a laid-back, cool look.

  • Plus-size linen blend trousers

    Searching for a wardrobe staple that's comfortable and stylish? New Look's wide-leg linen trousers feature a relaxed fit with an elasticated waist, and they'd be great for wearing to work.

  • Plus-size floral shirred dress

    There are so many plus-size floral dresses around for spring, and we're loving this ultra-feminine midi from Yours. It features a shirred waist with lightly puffed sleeves and a leg-split, making it the perfect piece for reaching for on sunny days throughout the season.

  • Plus-size denim jumpsuit

    Denim jumpsuits are everywhere right now, and we love them for creating a put-together, effortless look. Mango's one-piece is flattering on the figure with its extended sleeves and fitted waist, and it's available in blue and ecru.

  • Plus-size pinstripe blazer

    Having a blazer in your wardrobe is a saviour for whenever you're stuck on what to wear, and ASOS has nailed it with its navy pinstripe style. It can be teamed with jeans and a white top, or shop the matching trousers too to wear as a chic co-ord.

  • Plus-size striped sweater

    The chilly weather isn't over just yet, so a staple knit that can be teamed with dresses and trousers is a must. This quarter-zip striped sweater is guaranteed to be a springtime favourite.
