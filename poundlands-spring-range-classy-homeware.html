POUNDLAND'S biggest fan has revealed the latest range of homeware goodies they have in stock for spring.

Liyana Leena, 32, from Birmingham, wanted to see what was up for grabs so decided to check out her local Poundland store.

The content creator who goes by Liyana LifeStyles on YouTube was stunned to see the classy new range of homeware available.

And there were also lots of goodies to pick up for Mother's Day.

Liyana first found some incredible rattan baskets in black and beige that are perfect for £8 that are perfect for storing blankets in.

She noted: "I think the black one looks cheaper, the other one looks more expensive."

Liyana also found some adorable homeware bits for Easter including some decorative pastel Easter eggs and bunny ornaments for just £1.
