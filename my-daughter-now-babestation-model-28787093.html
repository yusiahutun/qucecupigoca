Levi is particularly pleased with how polite her daughter is with other staff and how empowered she is at such a young age.

She added: “I am just proud of how confident she is and she doesn’t let anything get to her.

“Watching the way she interacts with producers etc is great because she is so respectful and I think it’s really important. She is just a lovely person and she will chat to anyone.

“I couldn’t be prouder of her.”
