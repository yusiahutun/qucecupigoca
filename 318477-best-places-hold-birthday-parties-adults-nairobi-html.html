Places to hold birthday parties for adults in Nairobi

If you are stuck on where to hold your birthday party, then there is no need for panic. Below are a few birthday party venues in Nairobi that might serve the purpose of the day.

Que Pasa Bar & Bistro

Que Pasa Bar & Bistro is a perfect spot for that birthday girl or boy who is into sports. It has a warm and welcoming ambience. It is an ideal place for a night out to bond with friends over some whisky and beers in a sophisticated environment. Enjoy any of Chef Anthony’s platters from pizza to mussels.

Diguna High Ropes Challenge

If you are on a tight budget but still want to enjoy your birthday at an affordable price, then Diguna High Ropes Challenge is the perfect place. It gives you a mixture of thrill and adventure and an incredible fun time. It is situated at AIC Diguna, Rongai. It comprises of a course which has high ropes that you must walk on. It is an exciting place to be, and it costs Kshs 150 per person. To get there, board a matatu route 125/126 and alight at Maasai Lodge stage. You can decide to either walk the 2km distance to the venue or use the motorbikes at the stage.

The Balcony bar at Villa Rosa is a perfect chill point that for a small gathering for your celebration. The location has a wide variety of whisky. There’s a private cigar room for those who want to smoke to complete their day. This bar boasts of elegance and luxury.

The Nest at Tribe

The Nest is a great rooftop lounge that is highly recommended by those who visit there frequently. It brags of a great selection of food and drinks which come at relatively fair prices and the services do not disappoint. If you want a place for a small gathering for your birthday party then this venue will suit you well. It is on the 4th floor of the Tribe Hotel along Limuru Road.

Rockhouse

Inspired by the Mount Kenya historic Mau Mau caves, Rockhouse is a great location for your pool party. The beautiful scenery and the leafy suburbs of Karen give it an awesome ambience. It is situated off Ndovu Road Karen in Nairobi. Away from the city hustle, it would offer relaxation for those that want a quick get-way on Saturday afternoon for some nice drinks by the pool.

Villa Nerea

Do you want to rent a house for your birthday party? Villa Nerea is a perfect location if you don’t want anyone gate-crushing the occasion. With a fully furnished villa, a swimming pool, steam room, garden, an outdoor bar, sauna and a BBQ area on request it will give an experience like none other. It is located at Chalbi Drive House number 47 Lavington, Nairobi. If you are worried about the after party clean-up, then there is no need to panic because it will be taken care off by the hotel staff.

Rosedale gardens Kilimani

Rosedale boasts of lush green gardens and refreshing environment. It is a great place to hold your party because it offers great customer service. You can choose to either have it outside or indoors. It has great amenities such as ample parking, clean lavatory and even a powder room. Here you can host more than 300 guests. It is located at Rose Avenue, Off Dennis Pritt Road Kilimani.

Tamambo Karen Blixen Restaurant

Tamambo Karen Blixen Restaurant is a superb restaurant that is a romantic getaway for you and your spouse. It is suitable for a calm private dinner setting. The restaurant offers a menu that will leave you lost for choice and you can have to your fill. It is located along Karen Road, Nairobi

Caramel Restaurant

Caramel is, without a doubt, among the most romantic restaurants in Nairobi. It is an incredible place to surprise your loved one with a nice romantic dinner setting. It’s wine list and great menu is incomparable. It is located at ABC Place Westlands, Nairobi, Kenya.

Thai Chi at the Sarova Stanley Hotel

Does your significant other love the Asian style or want to try something new? You can surprise him or her with a nice romantic dinner at Thai Chi restaurant. Go Karting on Lang’ata Road

Does speeding give you a nice fulfilling thrill and you just want to let it all out? Then Go Karting on Lang’ata Road is the perfect place for you and your pals. The venue offers 10 minute sessions on the track that you and your pals can race and speed all you want.

Rock Climbing at Diamond Plaza

Ladies, it’s always nice to combine a little workout and some fun. Rock Climbing at Diamond Plaza has got you covered. Guess what, Tuesday night is the ladies night, and it’s Kshs 500 per session instead of the full price of Kshs 1000 on a normal occasion. It’s situated on 6th Floor, Old Tower, Diamond Plaza in Parklands.

Paintball Fury Limited

You don’t have to go to the real battlefield to get the army combat experience; you might as well do it on your birthday with a bunch of friends or family at Paintball Fury Limited. It is situated Karen and Lang’ata. The facility offers you the chance to enjoy the thrill of combat as teams fight to get the flag and advance to the opponent’s side without getting shot. After purchasing the paintballs, which should be a minimum of 100, you are provided with protective gear, including back and chest protector, overalls, and a headgear.
