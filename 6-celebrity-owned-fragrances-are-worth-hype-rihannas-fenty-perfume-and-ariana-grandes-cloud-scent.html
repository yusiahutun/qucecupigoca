6 celebrity-owned fragrances that are worth the hype: from Rihannas Fenty perfume and Ariana Gran

July 06, 2024
blog

Victoria Beckham is joining the world of celebrity perfumes! In late September, the Spice Girl announced the debut of her new fragrance line that will feature three unisex scents inspired by moments from her personal life.

In a world where a new celebrity brand, collaboration or product seems to launch every day, it’s easy to feel overwhelmed by celebrity-driven consumerism … but some of them indeed deliver top-quality products.

These fragrances are meant to capture the essence of their creators and reflect the evolving tastes of a new generation of perfume lovers. But which of them are truly worth the hype? From the charm of Rihanna to the timeless elegance of Sarah Jessica Parker and many others who have graced the perfumery world, these celebrity fragrances deserve a spot in your collection.

1. Cloud Eau de Parfum by Ariana Grande

Harmoniously blending floral notes, Ariana Grande’s Cloud steers clear of cloying, overwhelming sweetness. Since its debut in 2018, the scent has garnered an enthusiastic following among beauty editors and customers alike, reigning as the top-selling fragrance on multiple beauty platforms. In fact, the brand reported that a bottle was bought every 11 seconds in 2022.

The scent opens with a burst of lavender and juicy pear that’s both sweet and fresh, leading to a delicate blend of sweet coconut and creamy praline, adding a touch of warmth at the same time. The fragrance seamlessly adapts to any occasion, whether you’re wearing it for a casual day out or a special evening event.

2. Fenty Eau De Parfum by Rihanna

The scent is housed in an antique-inspired amber glass bottle designed to fit into Rihanna’s own palm, per Harper’s Bazaar. The genderless, boundary-breaking fragrance is a fruity, floral and spicy combo seamlessly blending creamy vanilla, fruity tangerine, sweet magnolia and earthy musk.

The complexity of Fenty is a testament to its artistry, a fragrance that continually surprises and delights as the day goes by.

3. Eilish Eau de Parfum by Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish’s debut fragrance is a harmonious blend of sweet, musky, fruity and woodsy notes mirroring her music and persona. It opens with an inviting burst of sugary vanilla, gradually revealing tonka bean, cocoa, musk and berry undertones – a captivating transformation that defies easy classification.

The scent has garnered immense online interest, ranking as the second-highest celebrity fragrance in online searches in 2022, second only to Ariana Grande’s collection, according to Statista. Its popularity stems from the fragrance’s unique and intoxicating blend, evoking a sense of mystery, sensuality and melancholy.

4. Love Rush by Paris Hilton

Love Rush encapsulates the essence of everlasting love. As her 29th perfume creation, it’s a testament to Hilton’s enduring passion for crafting scents that resonate with the heart.

Inspired by her own love story with husband Carter Reum and crafted ahead of their November 2021 wedding celebrations, Love Rush is an ode to romance and the promise of forever. With notes of Italian bergamot, white apricot nectar, lush gardenia, sandalwood, and vanilla orchids, it weaves a beautiful floral tapestry that many have become addicted to.

5. Lovely by Sarah Jessica Parker

Lovely is a scent born from SJP’s unique blend of three distinct aromas. The scent is a testament to Parker’s creative vision that’s captivated the hearts of generations, standing the test of time.

At first, it delivers a harmonious blend of lavender, mandarin orange, bergamot, martini and rosewood, evolving into patchouli, orchid and narcissus. The final lingering embrace is a fusion of musk, woodsy tones, cedar and white amber.
