They have just celebrated their seventh wedding anniversary together and now share four children.

But Dean McDermott has shown that he is totally devoted to his reality star wife Tori Spelling by getting a tattoo in her honor in an 'unbelievably intimate spot.'

Tattoo lover Tori took to Twitter to gush about her husband's latest inking by writing: 'OMG My husband@Deanracer just surprised me w/hottest Tori@ tattoo in an unbelievably intimate spot. I'm blown away #Tori Tatted 4 life.'

New Tattoo: Dean McDermott is spotted leaving a tattoo parlor in Toronto Canada last Thursday after getting his right arm inked for his wife Tori Spelling

Taped-up: Dean has his right arm covered-up after getting yet another tattoo on his right arm

Mysteriously Dean, 46, was spotted leaving what looked like a tattoo parlor in Canada last Thursday with a new tattoo underneath his covered-up right arm.

Wearing jeans, a blue t-shirt and clutching his jacket, the rugged reality star seemed to be in a little pain after getting the new tattoo.

But after his wife's latest confession it looks like he also had an etching done in a much more private area that his for Tori's eyes only.

That hurt! Dean seems to be in pain after getting his latest tattoo in Canada

The couple both share a love of tattoos and have several different designs on their bodies - Tori, 40, has some writing etched on the left side of her body while Dean collection includes an image of his famous wife above his elbow.

The reality stars have had their four children; Liam, Stella, Hattie and Finn, all in the space of six years,  the last two are even 'Irish twins' having been born in 2011 and 2012 respectively.

To celebrate their seventh anniversary together Dean got Tori a special spinning diamond ball ring designed from celebrity jewelery designer Neil Lane.

Inked lady: Tori Spelling shows off a scrolled tattoo while she sits poolside at The Parker Hotel in Palm springs while on holiday in 2011

Tattoo Guy: Dean has various Tattoos including this one of Tori's face above his elbow

The couple - who have experienced various highs and laws over the years - have just been hit with a $60 million law suit over claims Tori stole the idea for her latest reality show.

Three producers; Denny O'Neil Jr, Jake P. Hall and Charles W. Malcolm, claim her series sTORIbook Weddings was taken from their original idea.

They claim they came up with the idea for the couple to become wedding planners and help create their dream wedding.
