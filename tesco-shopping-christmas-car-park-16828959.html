Christmas shopping HORROR: 'Shoplifter' RAMS into cars outside Tesco store

June 22, 2024
blog

The carnage broke out in a Tesco carpark in Rickmansworth, Herts, at around 2pm on Sunday afternoon.

Video footage captures the shoppers in Christmas jumpers attempting to smash into a Citroen C4 – which has the alleged shoplifter inside – with trollies and golf clubs in a desperate attempt to stop them.

The car smashes into other vehicles a number of times and sustained damage to its passenger-side door, wing mirror, and rear window in the mayhem.

The driver appears to be frantically looking for a way out of the car park as people block the way, before finally smashing past a car and speeding off.

Police revealed the car was a rental vehicle and they were still hunting for the suspects.

“It’s believed the driver and passenger were involved in an incident inside the store moments before the collision, where a person was challenged by the store’s security after attempting to steal alcohol,” they said.

The force said one woman had suffered “serious” injuries and another man had been treated for more minor wounds at the scene.

The vehicle was said to be a blue Citroen C3 with a registration of KW18 HGK which “did not stop, but left the scene.”

"One person was taken to Watford General Hospital after sustaining a serious injury. Another person sustained slight injuries and also received hospital treatment,” police added.

Witness Terry Kennedy said he counted 12 police cars and four ambulances at the scene.

Kennedy said on Twitter the car park was taped off after the incident.
