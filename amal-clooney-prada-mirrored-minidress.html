Amal Clooney Wears a Mirrored Minidress That Costs Over $20K

June 12, 2024
blog

Amal Clooney stepped out in a look last night that had all eyes turned on her—and for good reason.

The human-rights lawyer enjoyed a night out in New York City on Wednesday, where she wore one of her most jaw-dropping outfits to date. The ensemble consisted of a $21,200 minidress hailing from Prada’s Fall/Winter 2022 collection. The incredible piece is made out triangle-shaped sequins (a nod to the house’s famous triangle logo) in black and reflective silver arranged into an intricate chainmail design. Clooney layered the dress over a low scoop-neck white tank top, which additionally bore a Prada triangle at the center of the bust. She topped it off with a sleek black blazer with long glossy lapels.

To accessorize, the lawyer coordinated with even more metallic elements, including a small metallic gold clutch and a pair of clear PVC pumps with a metallic silver toe and heel. She also added tiny diamond stud earrings.

Splash News

Clooney proved her style versatility by showing up to work earlier in the day in a completely different look.

She appeared in front of the 78th United Nations General Assembly to speak about the Declaration Against Arbitrary Detention in State-to-State Relations, which condemns arbitrary arrests by national governments and detaining foreign nationals to hold as leverage over a foreign government.

For the occasion, Clooney wore an elegant black-and-white tweed coat dress with matching black buttons down the center and a thin black belt cinched around her waist.
