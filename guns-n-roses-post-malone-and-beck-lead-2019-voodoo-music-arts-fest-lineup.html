Guns N Roses, Post Malone and Beck Lead 2019 Voodoo Music + Arts Fest Lineup

June 30, 2024
blog

NEW ORLEANS (CelebrityAccess) Guns N’ Roses, Post Malone and Beck have been announced as headliners for the Voodoo Music + Arts Experience when it returns to New Orleans for the 21st time in October.

Set to take place at NOLA’s City Park from October 25-27, the festival features more than 65 bands performing across four stages of music. Other artists announced for 2019 include Bassnectar, Brandi Carlile, The National, REZZ, Zhu, Young the Giant, Interpol, Sheck Wes, Bring Me the Horizon, Big Gigantic, Clairo, and Glitch Mob, among others.

In addition, the festival will also feature large-scale art installations, a selection of regionally sourced food vendors and in a nod to Halloween, an interactive haunted house.

For 2019, Voodoo is partnering with BYOBottle and will continue to provide free Hydration Stations throughout the park, which will help to reduce plastic waste from the fest. Last year over 106,000 plastic bottles were diverted through use of the festival’s hydration stations.

As well, the festival will employ compostable serving materials and compost stations as a part of its environmental sustainability efforts.

3-Day General Admission Tickets, 3-Day GA+ Tickets, 3-Day Loa VIP Tickets, and 3-Day Platinum Tickets will go on sale Thursday, June 6 at 10am CT.

Voodoo Music + Arts Experience is produced by Live Nation and sponsored by Paypal, Modelo Especial, Smirnoff Vodka, and New Belgium Brewing.
