Beyond Netflix: who are David and Victoria Beckhams 4 kids? Brooklyn runs a Facebook cooking show

May 19, 2024
blog

Speaking with Singapore Women’s Weekly, David, one of the most famous and revered football players of all time, said that his family is the most important thing in his life.

“Family, that’s what motivates me,” he said. “I’m dedicated to keep on proving to my children on how hard they have to work to be successful, to have a career, and to believe in themselves.”

Here’s everything you need to know about the Beckhams’ four kids.

1. Brooklyn Peltz Beckham, 24

David and Victoria welcomed Brooklyn Beckham on March 4, 1999, just a few months before they tied the knot.

Legendary singer Elton John, Canadian filmmaker David Furnish, and actor Elizabeth Hurley were later named as his godparents.

Although Brooklyn initially followed in his father’s footsteps as a promising young football player, he gave the sport up as a teen to pursue a career in modelling and later photography.

These days, Brooklyn’s primary focus is on cooking. He has his own Facebook Watch show Cookin’ with Brooklyn and told Variety last year that he one day wants to open a pub in Los Angeles.

Over the years, Brooklyn has also made headlines for his numerous relationships with famous women, including actor Chloë Grace Moretz and model Hana Cross, before ultimately finding lasting love with Nicola Peltz, whom he married in April 2022.

2. Romeo Beckham, 21

Born on September 1, 2002, Romeo Beckham, like his brother Brooklyn, was a promising young football star but gave up the sport in 2015 to pursue tennis following his release from British footie team Arsenal’s youth academy.

Romeo spent just one full season stateside before joining Brentford B on loan, with whom he signed a permanent contract earlier this year.

3. Cruz Beckham, 18

Cruz Beckham was born on February 20, 2005, in Madrid, Spain, at which time David played for Real Madrid.

Cruz began following in his mother Victoria’s footsteps as a musician from a young age. He took the stage with the Spice Girls when he was just three years old and by 11 he had released his debut single “If Every Day Was Christmas”.

Speaking to i-D magazine last year, Cruz said he had been working on new music with producer Poo Bear, who has previously produced tracks for Usher and Justin Bieber. “I’m taking my time seeing what happens,” he said, adding that he’s just focused on “just making the music I love”.

4. Harper Beckham, 12

The Beckhams’ youngest child Harper Beckham was born on July 10, 2011. In a Facebook video posted shortly after her birth, David said that Harper was named after Harper Lee, the author of Victoria’s favourite book To Kill a Mockingbird.

Posh and Becks often share photos of Harper on social media, whether it be playing football, or attending a Harry Styles concert with her dad. In an interview with Vogue Australia last year, Victoria said that Harper is a fan of both the Frozen and Harry Potter franchises. “She’s not one of these kids who are going out with a full face of make-up and a crop top,” Victoria said.
