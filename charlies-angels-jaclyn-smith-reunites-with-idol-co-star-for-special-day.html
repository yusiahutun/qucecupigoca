Charlie's Angels' Jaclyn Smith reunites with 'idol' co-star for 'special day'

August 30, 2024
blog

Charlie’s Angels star Jaclyn Smith has reunited with her “idol” co-star Kate Jackson for a special day and many fans found their reunion very heartwarming.

The Charlie’s Angels alum, 77, celebrated her son’s wedding and shared snippets from his big day on her Instagram. The special day was even more wonderful for long-time fans of the movie franchise as Jaclyn reunited with her beloved co-star Kate Jackson, 74.

Jaclyn Smith reunites with Charlie’s Angels co-star for “special day”

Charlie’s Angels stars Jaclyn Smith and Kate Jackson had the sweetest reunion at Jaclyn’s son’s wedding. Jaclyn’s son Gaston Richmond got married to his wife Bonnie Lane Richmond. 

To celebrate the wonderful occasion, Jaclyn shared an Instagram reel with the best snippets from the ceremony which featured a special moment with her co-star Kate.

“There is nothing like family, and ours grew this weekend!” Jaclyn wrote alongside the video. “I’m so endlessly proud of my son Gaston and am wishing him and Bonnie a lifetime of happiness.”

The reunion moment saw Jaclyn and Kate in great spirits as they chatted to each other and posed for a picture.

Jaclyn and Kate’s reunion is iconic

Jaclyn’s social media post was filled with congratulatory messages and full of appreciation for Jaclyn and Kate’s epic reunion.

“Beautiful!!” one fan wrote in the comments. “My heart stood still for Jaclyn next to Kate. My idols!”

“What an amazing event! Seeing you all look so happy is a beautiful thing. Seeing you and Kate together is incredible!!” someone else said.

Another fan added: “It was so nice to see Kate Jackson! The original Charlie’s Angels will always be my favorite!”

“Absolutely beautiful! Congratulations!” someone sele congratulated Jaclyn and added: “Really special to see Kate Jackson attend!”

Jaclyn Smith’s career

Jaclyn played the iconic character of Kelly Garrett in the TV series Charlie’s Angels in episodes between 1976 and 1981.

She made a brief cameo in the 2003 Charlie’s Angels: Full Throttle with Drew Barrymore, Cameron Diaz, and Lucy Liu, and also appeared in the most recent film, the 2019 Charlie’s Angels.

Some of her other roles include the 1984 series George Washington and the movies The Bourne Identity (1988) and Kaleidoscope (1990). Jacklyn received a Golden Globe nomination for her role in the 1981 film Jacqueline Bouvier Kennedy.
