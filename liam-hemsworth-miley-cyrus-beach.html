Liam Hemsworth Returned to the Beach Where He and Miley Cyrus First Kissed

February 05, 2024
blog

Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth shared their first kiss in 2009 while filming The Last Song on Tybee Island, Georgia. Eight years later, the actor returned to the same location, this time to film another movie.

Hemsworth was spotted on the beach at Tybee Island—shirtless, no less—while taking a break from filming his new project, Killerman. Unlike The Last Song, this flick is an action-thriller, where the actor plays a "money launderer suffering from amnesia after a freak accident during a deal gone wrong," according to Variety.

We like to imagine that in between all those action scenes, Hemsworth secretly pays tribute to his and Miley's first moments together.

It turns out that Miley and Liam's romantic first kiss wasn't even planned. Hemsworth revealed that the smooch was improvised while they were filming that scene on the shore.

"It wasn't in the script," he told E! in 2010. "I read the script and it just said, 'frolicking on the beach.' So [the director] was yelling things like, 'Splash here! Push her over! Jump on his back!' But then she yelled, 'Kiss him!'?"

The actor recalled that real-life nerves kicked in while the cameras were rolling. "It was good because you can get nervous with the first kiss," he added. "But there was no time to get nervous. And it's not like you can stop and say, 'No!'?"

And the rest is history.
