Roger Ailes' New Jersey mansion sells for $1.65m

September 04, 2024
blog

Late Fox News head Roger Ailes' New Jersey mansion has been sold for $1.65 million only weeks after his passing, but his family, who had been desperately trying to sell off the property for months, will take a $200,000 loss on the sale.

Ailes purchased the four-bedroom, six-bathroom, 3,502 square-foot mansion located in Cresskill, New Jersey back in 2000 for $1.85 million.

He had originally listed the property on March 9 for a $2.25 million, but then slashed to price to $1,995,000, weeks later. 

Most recently, the former CEO of Fox News cut another $345,000 from the asking price on May 3.
