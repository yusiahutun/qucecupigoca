Stunning contestant gets completely naked on Argentinas Strictly Come Dancing

April 10, 2024
blog

Dancing for a Dream is, roughly speaking, Argentina’s equivalent to the beloved British gameshow – albeit with a decidedly lower threshold for the celebrity status required to enter.

The show also follows a slightly different format to its cousins in other countries, with one series airing four or five nights a week for a period of up to nine months due to the number of contestants.

Such vast numbers of competitors can often make it difficult to remember each individual performance – although that wasn’t a problem that Cinthia Fernandez ever had to worry about.

The Argentinean beauty gave the raunchiest performance in the show's – if not television's – history during series seven back in 2011.

Coupled with her greased-up accomplice, the gymnast-turned-model launched into an X-rated routine in which she stripped down to her underwear, removed her g-string and dry-humped her partner.

Viewers were shocked following the unexpectedly racy display – but not shocked enough to eliminate Fernandez.

She made it through to the following round, putting in an impressive performance despite having both of her parents in attendance.

Almost 800,000 people have seen the clip since it was posted on YouTube.

“Wow, the South Americans really know how to do Strictly Come Dancing,” read one comment.

Another said: “I would actually consider watching Strictly if they always showed this kind of dancing!”
