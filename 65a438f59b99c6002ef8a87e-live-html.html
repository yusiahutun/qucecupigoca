Who won this year's pageant and every highlight of the night

March 18, 2024
blog

The finals for Miss America 2024 will take place tonight at 7:00 pm ET, live from the Walt Disney Theater at the Dr. Phillips Center in Orlando, Florida. The Sunday Jan. 14 event will gather 51 contestants from all 50 US states and the District of Columbia.

The newly crowned Miss America 2024 will receive a $50,000 tuition scholarship, as well as a spectacular crown which will be handed by Miss America 2023 winner Grace Stanke of Wisconsin. Hanley House of North Carolina, who was crowned on Saturday as Miss America's Teen 2024, was awarded a $25,000 scholarship or the option of a four-year full ride at the University of Alabama.

While the pageant will not be broadcast on TV this year, it can be streamed through the official Miss America website. Voting will also be available for the event, which will continue to reinforce the values it is looking for in its contestants: talent, communication skills and a commitment to social impact.
