Her career has seen its most fruitful year yet and Octavia Spencer is currently preparing for a possible win at the Academy Awards on Sunday.

And The Help star has now revealed that she has an interesting way of celebrating her success in mind.

The 41-year-old told Parade magazine that she intends to buy herself a breast lift.

A present to herself: Octavia Spencer wants to celebrate her success with a breast lift

She said: ‘I am going to get my boobs lifted. That's going to be my first gift.

‘I figured going into my forties I want my boobs where they were when I was 17.’

However the actress admitted that while she is keen to go under the knife, it won’t be happening just yet.

Success: Octavia says the breast lift would be a 'gift' to herself and she hopes to have the surgery later this year

She told the publication: ‘Right after the Oscars I start a Diablo Cody movie. We're taping out of the country for four months so I wouldn't be back here until about August.

‘I would want to do this around November. Then that way I could just relax until January.’

The star is up for the Best Supporting Actress in a Motion Picture category at Sunday’s Oscars for her role in box-office smash The Help.

Oscar nominee: The actress is up for an Academy Award for her role in The Help

And surgery is not the only measure the star is taking in a bid to improve her figure.

She revealed to Us Weekly in January that she has been working out with a personal trainer.

She told the magazine: ‘I'm not trying to be a thin mint. I'm just trying to be healthy and look good for me. So another 15 pounds and I will be fine.’
