Kayla Maisonet came into the public spotlight after her role in the Disney Channel’s children’s TV show Dog with a Blog.

She appeared on this show from 2012 to 2015 for 20 episodes. She also appeared in another Disney show, Stuck in the Middle, for 57 episodes from 2016 to 2018. 

Kayla Maisonet Dating, Boyfriend

Kayla is in a relationship with Nathaniel Potvin. They have been dating from around 2017.

The couple frequently posts pictures of each other on their Instagram accounts. They are also seen going out together.

Both of them starred in the Nickelodeon series, Haunted Hathaways; Kayla appeared in three episodes in 2013, whereas Nathaniel appeared in only one episode in 2014. 

More On Nathaniel

Nathaniel (full name - Nathaniel James Potvin), just like his girlfriend, is an actor. He has appeared in shows such as CSI: Crime Scene Investigation in 2014, Mech-X4 from 2016, Alexa & Katie from 2018 to 2019, and Five Points from 2018 to 2019. 

Nathaniel is more experienced in the show business as he has 19 credits as an actor, whereas Kayla has only 10.

Parents & Family

Kayla is the daughter of Angelo Maisonet and Amanda Maisonet. She grew up alongside her elder brother, Justin Maisonet.

Having been born in New York, her family moved a few times. First, they moved to New Jersey. Then, after living a few years in New Jersey, they moved to Los Angeles during her teenage years.

Career

After rising to prominence from her roles in Disney’s shows, she transitioned to being a mainstream actress with her work in the show, Crazy Ex-Girlfriend, in 2018. Then, in 2019, she starred in the ABC sitcom, Speechless.
