Patrick Mahomes Bio

Patrick Mahomes’ birth name is Patrick Lavon Mahomes II his father shares the same name. He was born on September 17, 1995, in Tyler Texas. Born at a time when his father was already a professional athlete Patrick Mahome had a fun childhood by throwing balls for MLB greats like Derek Jeter and training with Alex Rodriguez. Raised in Whitehouse, Texas, Mahomes attended Whitehouse High School where he played three sports: football, baseball, and basketball.

In his final year, Mahomes was outstanding in football and baseball. In one game in his final year, Mahomes threw a no-hitter with 16 strikeouts. This and many other achievements have put him on the radar of many high-level MLB teams. At the 2014 Major League draft the Detroit Tigers picked him up in the 37th round but Mahomes had already signed up with Texas Tech who offered him a scholarship to play football. His father a professional baseball player was surprised by his decision but still supported him. Before university Mahomes doubted his ability as a footballer but his mother Randi encouraged him.

Mahomes played as a reserve quarterback in the first year before moving to the starting QB in the second year. His love of baseball attracted him to the university baseball team where he occasionally played as a relief pitcher. From his first year, Mahomes turned to football and the results were immediately visible.
