What is Port Protection all about?

Port Protection is a reality television show that follows a group of individuals living in a remote, fog-shrouded community of Port Protection. Port Protection is in the northwest corner of Prince Wales Island, Alaska. The region is surrounded by the freezing waters of the North Pacific.

Port Protection has approximately 100 residents that push the limits of survival by hunting, fishing, trapping, growing their food and building shelter. On top of this, the region has no government or law enforcement, making their living conditions even more dangerous.

The cast and people living in the region consider the risk is worth the big reward of a world with community security without the limitations of bureaucracy.
