Eva Longoria Is a Vision in a Bright Pink Shorts Suit

blog

Eva Longoria was a vision in pink while out to dinner in Los Angeles.

The actress was spotted Saturday arriving at hot spot Catch Steak in an elevated summer shorts suit in the season's most popular color, beaming as she was caught by the cameras.

Longoria paired the hot pink blazer and matching high-waisted shorts from Skies Are Blue with a simple white cropped tank top and clear pointed-toe heels with white soles. She accessorized with layered gold, silver, and diamond necklaces and bracelets, as well as a gold watch and oversized hoop earrings. She also carried her favorite new accessory: her Casa Del Sol canvas tote bag. (Longoria is the cofounder of the L.A.-favorite tequila brand.)

The Desperate Housewives star's hair was styled up in a high ponytail with loose, wavy strands framing her face, and she wore natural rosy makeup with inky black eyeliner to accent her eyes.
