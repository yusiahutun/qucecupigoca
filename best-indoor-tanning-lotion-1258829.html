    How to Use An Indoor Tanning Lotion?

    Let us break it down for you on how to use these lotions like a pro-

    1. Shower And Exfoliate: Start with a lukewarm shower and a gentle exfoliation. This helps remove dead skin cells and creates a smooth canvas for the lotion. Extra attention is such a need for rough areas like elbows and knees.

    2. Pat Dry: No need to go all rough with the towel — just pat dry, fam! Be gentle and keep a bit of that moisture on your skin.

    3. Time for Lotion: Evenly spread that indoor tanning lotion all over, squad! Go easy on your hands and feet to dodge those uneven patches. And remember, wash those hands after, unless you're going for the tanned palm look! 

    4. Time to Catch Those Rays: Get in that tanning bed or soak up some sun outdoors. Don't forget to stick to the recommended time for your sesh.  

    5. Post-tan Vibes: Hydrate that skin with some moisturizer love! Also as you know tanning can be a bit drying, so keep your skin all happy and glowy. 

    6. Sun-Savvy: If you're catching those rays outdoors, slap on some zinc-based sunscreen, especially for your lovely face. Indoor tanning lotions don't have your back when it comes to sun protection. 

    Boom! That's the lowdown on using indoor tanning lotion — it's all about the prep, that smooth application, and post-tan self-care. Get set to slay that bronzed goddess vibe with confidence. You're going to look absolutely on fire!

    How do indoor tanning lotions work?

    It only works best after you take a bath with lukewarm water, your skin gets ready to get the bronze glow. Avoid using it directly after work. Unwind yourself, relax then take a shower to rev up the glow.

    What is the best tanning bed lotion to get dark fast?

    Level 4 tanning beds and beyond. They're like the Ferrari of tanning! These bad boys offer crazy fast tanning times and, here's the kicker, next to no UVB rays. That means you're getting that wicked dark tan without even a whisper of a sunburn.

    How long before indoor tanning should you apply tanning lotion?

    Get that lotion game on point! Make sure you apply it at least 20-30 minutes before your tanning sesh. This gives it the right amount of time to cozy up to your skin and get absorbed.

    How long can you keep indoor tanning lotion?

    Tanning lotions come with a shelf life of around 12 months. So, no need to hoard them forever! Use them while they're at their best and keep that glow game strong.

    How long after indoor tanning can you shower?

    Wait for at least 8-10 hours before taking a shower.

    Is it better to shower before or after tanning?

    Well, the drill is that the longer you wait, the better result you get!

    Can I use indoor tanning lotions if I have sensitive skin or allergies?
