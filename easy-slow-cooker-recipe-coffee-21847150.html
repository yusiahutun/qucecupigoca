Easy slow cooker recipe for coffee-flavoured fruit cake with three ingredients

May 26, 2024
blog

Since the UK lockdown, pretty much everyone on Instagram has been sharing videos of their baking skills.

It might have made you feel a little bit envious and inspire you to jump on the bandwagon to make your own cake.

So if you want to take it to another level, here's a simple recipe for a coffee-flavoured fruit cake – and it only needs three ingredients.

A cook shared the method on Facebook in the Slow Cooker Recipes & Tips group.

All you need for the treat is iced coffee (750ml), mixed dried fruit (1kg) and self-raising flour (265g).

Method:

To start, soak fruit in iced coffee overnight. The following morning, mix in the flour and pour into a greased cake tin and place in the slow cooker.

Pour water up the sides of the cake pan in the slow cooker and place a tea towel underneath before putting it on low for 7 hours.

Of course it takes a while for it to bake but it's still that easy.

And it means that you've got something to look forward to in the evening.
