Best for bleached and damaged hair

Olaplex No 6 Bond Smoother

1

Best for bleached and damaged hair

Olaplex No 6 Bond Smoother

Credit: Sephora

Olaplex might have found fame with its phenomenally popular pre-shampoo treatment, but we think this styling cream is equally as good. Ideal for colour-treated hair that tends to break at the ends, it'll shield from frizz while treating the damaged sections of each fibre, offering both instant and long-term benefits.

Use less than you think you'll need: a small amount goes a long way.

2

Best for a salon-style blow-dry

Oribe Royal Blowout Heat Styling Spray

2

Best for a salon-style blow-dry

Oribe Royal Blowout Heat Styling Spray

Credit: Space NK

Oribe's blow-dry spray is easily one of the best new launches we've tried this year. Really, it ticks every box: blow-dry time is reduced, shine is enhanced, and frizz is almost entirely blocked (take it from a Bazaar beauty editor with eternally puffy lengths). Yes, it's indulgent, but it'll deliver on every styling demand.

Best for fluffy hair

Shu Uemura Art of Hair Ashita Supreme Serum

3

Best for fluffy hair

Shu Uemura Art of Hair Ashita Supreme Serum

Now 10% Off

Credit: Lookfantastic

Shu Uemura's Ashita Supreme line is designed to balance both scalp and strands, but this serum is focused specifically on dry, frizz-prone lengths. Smooth it in pre-drying, and you'll find your hair looks much sleeker, reducing the need to reach for the hot tools. Lightweight and milky, it's a good choice for finer hair types.

4

Best for straightener addicts

Virtue Frizz Block Smoothing Spray

4

Best for straightener addicts

Virtue Frizz Block Smoothing Spray

Credit: Space NK

Virtue's offering is one for the most stubbornly unruly hair. This watery, alcohol-free spray may feel lightweight, but it works brilliantly when it comes to shielding hair from humidity, and even a sudden downpour.

Infused with the brand's signature keratin extract, it'll bind itself to roughed-up sections of each hair fibre, aiding repair with long-term use. Heat is required to activate the formula, so mist it in before blow-drying and styling.

5

Best silicone-free option

Living Proof No Frizz Smooth Styling Spray

5

Best silicone-free option

Living Proof No Frizz Smooth Styling Spray

Credit: Space NK

Living Proof's No Frizz line gains two new members this month. The original Smooth Styling Cream has been tweaked into a lightweight spray and a heavy-duty serum, so now caters for all hair types and textures. All three contain a patented polymer that acts as a lightweight shield from both water and oil, stopping moisture from penetrating without falling back on silicones.

Best for textured hair

Dizziak Deep Conditioner

6

Best for textured hair

Dizziak Deep Conditioner

Credit: Cult Beauty

Best for fine hair

Best for dry hair

Sisley Hair Rituel Precious Hair Care Oil

8

Best for dry hair

Sisley Hair Rituel Precious Hair Care Oil

Sisley's sublime hair oil creates a weightless veil over hair, smoothing each cuticle and boosting glossiness tenfold. The luxurious blend of passion fruit, shea, cotton and moringa oils smells incredible, too. A truly indulgent investment.

Best for results that last

Color WOW Dream Coat Supernatural Spray

9

Best for results that last

Color WOW Dream Coat Supernatural Spray

Credit: Lookfantastic

The science behind Color Wow's cult Dream Coat spray is pretty clever: it's based around heat-activated droplets that crosslink together to form an invisible hydrophobic (water-resistant) coating on your hair. The result is frizz-free hair with a high-shine finish. The effects will even survive a couple of shampoos.

Best for touch-ups

Best for style prep

14

Best for style prep

Aveda Smooth Infusion Style-Prep Aveda Smoother

Credit: Lookfantastic
