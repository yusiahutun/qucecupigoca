Haylie Duff reveals she chose breastfeeding over losing baby weight

July 13, 2024
blog

Haylie Duff has opened up about her 'struggle' to get her body back after giving birth to her first child.

The celebrity chef, who has appeared in her own cooking series The Real Girl's Kitchen, welcomed daughter Ryan in May last year with her fiancé Matt Rosenberg.

In an interview with Wonderwall.com, the 30-year-old sister of Hilary Duff revealed she decided to scale back on dieting and exercise after she discovered it affected her ability to breastfeed. 

'Struggle': Haylie Duff, pictured left in December last year and right in April just before giving birth, has spoken about her body after baby

She explained: 'I definitely do not feel like I'm looking amazing. 

'I had sort of an interesting situation when I was nursing where if I was working out too much or cutting my calories, my milk supply really went down.

'So for me, it was a struggle because I really wanted to focus on losing weight and getting my body back.

'But at the end of the day, I had to sit down and make the decision that meant the most to me - and that was breastfeeding my baby. 

'So I just got to the point where I was like, 'I don't care as much about [losing weight]. I'm gonna focus on doing what's right for her for a while. 

New mother: The celebrity chef with daughter Ryan and fiance Matt Rosenberg in December 

'It's coming off. It's a slow and steady process. The plus side is I have a beautiful, healthy little girl.'

Her sister Hilary, who has a three-year-old son Luca, was also candid about her body struggles after giving birth, admitting that it took her a year to get back in shape. 'It was harder than I thought it was going to be,' Hilary said in 2013.

Haylie and Matt got engaged in April 2014 but put their wedding plans on hold after they found out she was pregnant.
