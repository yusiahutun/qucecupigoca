Model Maddie White goes viral on TikTok for cutting up a $2,100 Prada jumpsuit

blog

A Los Angeles model has caused quite the stir online after cutting up a $2,100 Prada jumpsuit and turning it into a skirt, top, and legwarmers.

Maddie White, who was born in the UK but grew up in Los Angeles, California, stunned TikTok users after taking a pair of scissors to the designer poplin one-piece.

The 27-year-old, who has racked up more than four million followers on the platform, effortlessly turned the jumpsuit into a new ensemble.

The mesmerizing clip has so far been viewed more than 3.2 million times - but not everyone seems convinced by the transformation, with some branding it as 'sacrilege.'

'I don't know how I got from filming silly little TikToks in my apartment to here,' she added.

Maddie slips into the $2,100 gray poplin jumpsuit and continues: 'Okay, this is the jumpsuit - very chic, very utilitarian.

'I think we need to make it a little more me.'

Maddie then takes a deep breath as she waves scissors in the sign of the cross and tells fans to 'say a prayer.'

The influencer starts to cut the jumpsuit into a crop top and trousers before also then removing the legs.

But she stresses 'we don't waste in this house' before using the discarded trousers as legwarmers.
