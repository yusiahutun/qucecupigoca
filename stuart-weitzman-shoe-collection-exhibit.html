Stuart Weitzman's Private Shoe Collection

April 01, 2024
blog

Stuart Weitzman is sharing his personal shoe collection for the first time—and it's every shoe lovers dream. The Walk This Way exhibition, housed at the New-York Historical Society, explores how shoes have changed from their utilitarian purpose to become representations of culture— highlighting key parts of women's history and expressing complicated meanings of femininity, power, and aspiration for women and men alike.

Walk This Way: Footwear from the Stuart Weitzman Collection of Historic Shoes highlights 100 pairs of shoes from the iconic designer’s extensive private collection—including one pair that's literally worth a million dollars and dripping in diamonds.

On view from now through October 8, this is one exhibition that shoe lovers don't want to miss.
