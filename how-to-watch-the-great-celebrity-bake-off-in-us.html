The Great Celebrity Bake Off for Stand Up To Cancer is a spinoff of The Great British Bake Off (or The Great British Baking Show in the US). The celebrity competition raises money for Stand Up To Cancer UK, a joint national fundraising campaign between Channel 4 and Cancer Research UK that has raised more than £113m to fund life-saving cancer research.

    Who are The Great Celebrity Bake Off 2024 hosts and judges? Noel Fielding and Alison Hammond returned to host this year’s The Great Celebrity Bake Off. They are joined by the show’s longtime judges, Paul Hollywood and Prue Leith.

    This marks Hammond’s first time co-hosting the Stand Up To Cancer Bake Off special. “I’m totally in awe of this year’s celebrity bakers, all putting their baking skills, or in some cases lack of, under nationwide scrutiny to raise money for Stand Up To Cancer,” Hammond said in a statement, according to Yahoo UK. “And as someone who proved to be something of a baking genius [sic] when I baked in the tent a few years ago, I was delighted to be able to show them the ropes and where the oven doors were.”

The 85-year-old chef will take a step back from the spinoff, according to a spokesperson from the show’s production company, Love Productions. “Prue will be joining Paul [Hollywood] as usual to judge The Great British Bake Off, but will be stepping back from The Great Celebrity Bake Off for Stand Up To Cancer,” the official statement to Digital Spy read.
