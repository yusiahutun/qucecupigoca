Rihanna Embraces Her Wild Side in a Cheetah-Print Minidress and Fur Coat

March 18, 2024
blog

Rihanna doesn’t just cash in on trends—she sets them. Every look serves as a reminder that she does things better than anyone else, simply because she’s that girl.

With animal print making a comeback (which can be credited in part to the “mob wife aesthetic” taking over the internet this winter), it was just a matter of time before the “Love on the Brain” singer put us to shame, and on Friday, she delivered. Rihanna was spotted attending best friend Melissa Forde’s birthday celebration at the Nice Guy restaurant in Los Angeles, and she fittingly brought the party with her outfit.

The Hollywood Curtain/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

The Fenty founder turned up the spice in a teeny-tiny cheetah-print minidress that she wore low on her chest to reveal her black push-up bra, paired with sheer black tights and matching velvet pinched-toe heels. It only got better from there—Rihanna was far from done embracing her wild side. Atop her dress, she wore a flowy, luxe cheetah-print coat that featured a dramatic shaggy black fur collar and ultra-wide curly black fur cuffs. She kept the theme going with a fluffy cheetah-print purse with brassy gold hardware, in a light caramel brown.

Ever the maximalist, Rihanna added her signature layers of diamonds in the form of several stacked necklaces and a diamond-encrusted statement ring. She accented further with a pair of tortoiseshell shades with yellow-tinted lenses; wore bronzy, glowy makeup, complete with glossy muted red lips; and styled her golden locks down in a blowout.

Rihanna’s bombshell look comes on the heels of her showing us how the pantsless trend is done. She enjoyed a Color Me Mine date night with beau A$AP Rocky in a cobalt-blue Los Angeles Dodgers baseball cap, a graphic baby tee tucked into a white and blue striped button-up, layered with a black zip-up hoodie from R13, and paired with sexy black and nude Balenciaga fishnet pantaboots.
