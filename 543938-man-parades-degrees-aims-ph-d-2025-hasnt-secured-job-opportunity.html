Man Parades His Degrees as He Aims for PhD in 2025, Says He Hasn't Secured Job Opportunity

July 28, 2024
blog
  • Social media users have reacted to a viral photo of a man in a suit and his graduation gown flaunting his certificates
  • Facebook user Alson Manyama flaunted his certificates as he noted he was looking forward to doing his PhD in 2025
  • The post went viral and sparked mixed reactions from social media users who expressed their opinions in the comment section

A young man has taken to social media to share his bitter-sweet story in the hope of finding assistance.

Alson Manyama shared a photo of him in his graduation gown, flaunting his certificates.

The internet personality had his certificates scattered around and about on the grass as he held some in his hand.

Donning graduation gown on suit

"Alson Manyama parades all his degrees as he aims for a PhD in 2025. He says he hasn't secured a job opportunity," Facebook user Lemiso Media House shared.

Manyama looked smart in his fitting suit, which included a tie and a white shirt. He also wore brown shoes to finish off his flashy appearance.

In one photo, he stooped to look at the certificates, holding one in his hand, while in another, he leaned on a post.

He looked at one certificate in another as he walked around the scattered certificates on the green grass.

Social media reactions

His heartbreaking story went viral, sparking mixed reactions from social media users who reshared his photos and plight.

Eric Omondi to secure jobs for jobless graduates

Earlier, Eric Omondi vowed to secure employment for every jobless graduate who would attend his meeting, which took place on Tuesday, March 26.

Several graduates attended the meeting and formed an office to streamline their job search with his support.

He called on Kenyans to contribute towards the office’s rent, fostering a spirit of unity.
