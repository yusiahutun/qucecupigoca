Teresa Palmer says she's been breastfeeding non-stop for nearly a DECADE

August 21, 2024

Teresa Palmer has revealed the sobering reality of raising four children below the age of six.

The Australian actress, who shares children Bodhi, nine, Forest, six, Poet, four, and Prairie, two, with husband Mark Webber, revealed she's been breastfeeding non-stop since becoming a mother in 2014. 

'I'm on day 3367 or something so my boobs are pretty adaptable,' joked Teresa in an interview with Marie Claire Australia this week. 

Palmer breastfed her older children while pregnant and regularly nursed two children at once - a practice called 'tandem feeding'.

'My boobs grow double the size by the end of the day', she added.
