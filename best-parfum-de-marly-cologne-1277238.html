    How to Select the Best Parfums de Marly Cologne?

    Here are a few aspects that you should consider before splurging on the best Parfums de Marly cologne: 

    Notes: Make sure you check the top, middle, and base notes of any of the best PDM fragrances that you are planning to invest in. Each note has a unique scent profile which will help you understand the overall fragrance of the cologne. For a fresh, floral, and fruity aroma, pick colognes that contain top notes of bergamot, mandarin orange, green apple, lavender, thyme, saffron, cypress, and cashmere wood. If you are more inclined towards investing in perfumes that are woody and musky, ensure that you choose a cologne with spicy heart notes of pink pepper and cinnamon and base notes of sandalwood, musk, amber, and patchouli. 

    Fragrance Family: Understand the fragrance family of the colognes so that you can align it well with your needs and preferences. Colognes belong to a variety of fragrance families like fruity, floral, citrusy, aquatic, and woody. For special occasions, you may pick the one that is woody and rich. For daytime wear, you can choose more floral or fruity fragrances.

    Season: Next check the season that the cologne suits the best — spring, winter, summer, or fall. Colognes that are lighter and refreshing are best suitable for summer or hot weather and spring whereas warm and spicy colognes are ideal to be applied during the winter season. 

    Longevity: Last but not least, ensure that you get your hands on the best Parfums de Marly for summer, winter, spring, and fall that are long-lasting. When we say longevity, we mean how long will the scent last on your skin. In this regard too, it all depends on your preference. Some might prefer long-lasting fragrances that have a strong and overwhelming projection while some may like a more subtle scent.

    How to Apply a Cologne?

    Following are a few tips for the proper application of a cologne: 

    - Make sure your skin is cleansed and moisturized as cologne tends to last longer this way.   

    - You can apply the cologne on your pulse points and areas that are naturally warm like elbows, wrists, ears, forearms, chest, neck, etc. 

    - You may apply it immediately after taking a shower as your pores are open ensuring better absorption of the scent. 

    - Hold the bottle 3 inches away to avoid skin irritation or staining of clothes. 

    - Start with a light application. Refrain from spraying too much in one go.

    - You may re-apply as and when needed.
