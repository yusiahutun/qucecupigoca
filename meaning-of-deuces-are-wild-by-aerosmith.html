Meaning of Deuces Are Wild by Aerosmith

April 08, 2024
blog

The song "Deuces Are Wild" by Aerosmith is a rich masterpiece that delves into the complexities of love and relationships. As we explore the lyrics of this rock classic, we uncover a multitude of unexpected and imaginative themes that all tie back to the overarching theme of the song - the wild and unpredictable nature of love.The opening verse sets the stage for the intense emotions that permeate the song. The singer declares his love for someone by looking into their big brown eyes. These eyes, described as hypnotic, have the power to say the things nobody else dares to say. This highlights the theme of emotional vulnerability and the profound connection that can exist between two individuals.Moving on to the chorus, we encounter the standout lyric, "I love you 'cause your deuces are wild, girl, like a double shot of lovin' so fine." Here, the theme of unpredictability is introduced. The mention of "deuces" symbolizes the wild and untamed aspect of the person the singer is addressing. This lyric suggests that the singer is drawn to their partner's unpredictable nature, finding it exciting and invigorating. It celebrates the passionate and intense love they share, comparing it to a double shot of a potent elixir.In the second verse, the lyrics take an unexpected turn. The singer feels a sense of familiarity and deja vu with the person they are addressing. This introduces the theme of destiny and a connection that transcends time and space. Despite the feeling of being apart or separated, the other person is always near, present in the singer's dreams. The sudden interruption by someone slamming a door signifies the fleeting nature of these dreamlike moments, reminding us that even the strongest connections can be disrupted.Another standout lyric arises with the mention of "Screamin' mama's little baby loves shortnin' bread." This playful and lighthearted reference adds a touch of innocence and nostalgia to the song. It evokes the theme of longing for a simpler time, reminiscent of the carefree days of childhood. The juxtaposition of this innocent reference within a song filled with passion and intensity further emphasizes the multifaceted nature of love.Throughout the song, the recurring theme of addiction becomes evident. The singer confesses that they get "stoned" on the person they love, which is the "best reason why." This lyric explores the addictive nature of love and the powerful hold it can have over an individual. Love, as portrayed in the song, becomes a source of intoxication and euphoria, reflecting the overwhelming emotions experienced when deeply connected to someone.In conclusion, "Deuces Are Wild" by Aerosmith is a captivating song that delves into the wild and unpredictable nature of love. The lyrics paint a vivid picture of emotional vulnerability, unpredictability, destiny, and addiction. These unexpected and imaginative themes all intertwine to create a song that resonates with listeners on a profound level. It captures the essence of the roller coaster ride that love often is, with its twists, turns, and intense emotions. Through its masterful storytelling, Aerosmith reminds us that love, in all its wildness, is a force to be reckoned with.
