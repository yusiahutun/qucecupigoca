Holly, who has just returned to screens after a break from TV, has revealed she has more than one trick up her sleeve when it comes to wooing Dan. One of her secrets to a successful date night with Dan is going completely nude under a coat.

The mother-of-three admitted she likes to wear heels in the bedroom with her husband Dan Baldwin. In 2019, she told Now magazine: "I love wearing new shoes during sex – and not just because it’s kinky.

"I’m not a foot fetishist or anything, I have found the best way to break in new shoes is to wear them during sex. It’s become a sort of rule whenever I get a new pair of shoes.

I always wear them in the bedroom - during the sex act - before I take them outside. Every girl loves a new pair of six-inch heels and I’m no different."

Kinky outfit

Holly never fails to sparkle with her gorgeous gowns at showbiz events, not to mention her dazzling Dancing on Ice dresses. But when it comes to date nights with her hubby, she likes to do something a little naughtier.

She previously said: “For a big date night, I wear a coat with nothing on underneath."

Saucy games with hubby

Holly has also admitted to playing cheeky games in the bedroom with Dan. She said: "I do enjoy saucy games with my hubby."

She hasn't elaborated on just what is involved, but it seems some roleplay may be involved. The star admitted: "I also like the sexy secretary look."

Wedding day surprise

Holly's wedding was gorgeous, but she opted for something a little more daring underneath the gown.

She said on her website Wylde Moon: "It's obviously very important to get your underwear right under your wedding dress, and even more so with a backless dress.

"My amazing dressmaker had a vintage pattern for a bunny girl outfit. She adapted a 1960's original bunny girl corset by cutting the back so low, it didn't show."

Holly then joked: "She even made me a fluffy tail – but I’m not sure that ever saw the light of day!"

She's never had a 'fountain' during sex

Holly admitted that she has never 'squirted' during sex, during an extremely explicit episode of Celebrity Juice. Host Keith Lemon decided to question her about her bedroom antics.

"Do you like making babies?" asked Keith. Holly replied: "I love making babies!"

Keith then took things further, asking if Holly can make a "fountain". "Do you mean squirting?" asked an embarrassed Holly. "I have not done that. I don’t think it’s real."

Racy chatter

Holly keeps some aspects of her sex life very private but she's happy to talk about it with pals. During a segment on This Morning in 2022, the star got rather embarrassed when asked a sex question about her and her husband.

Holly and her then co-star Phillip Schofield were having a chat with guest Dawn Ward at the time about whether it was true that a third of all women are uninterested in sex. Dawn said she bonks her hubby at least three times a week.

She then asked an embarrassed Holly how often she gets it on. Holly replied coyly: "I am not part of this. This is not something anyone needs to know. I can be honest with my friends but I am not going to talk about this on air."
