'Worst bar in Benidorm' where 'drugs offered and bouncers batter people for no reason'

A bar in Benidorm has been slammed as the holiday destination’s “worst” after a slew of events and accusations.

The Red Lion in Benidorm, Spain has been subject to a variety of allegations with claims about brutal bouncers and seedy drug dealing.

Some of the allegations are quite serious with a slew of reviews on TripAdvisor slating the late-night spot.

Allegations of violence from bouncers are common and have been lodged periodically over the last few years.

Another one, titled “Such a disgraceful place, worst bar in Benidorm stay well away!!!” from January 2020 said: “This place by far is a really dangerous place the manager and the bouncers think it’s ok to knock a person out for no reason and leave him on the road!!”

In November 2018 footage emerged of what appeared to be a fight outside of the Red Lion.

The Sun reported that Brits opted to boycott the boozer on account of the bouncers being “bullies”.

The hectic scrap is shocking with massive punches thrown and people screaming at the chaos.

While it is not clear what led to the incident it sparked an outcry, with footage showing one bloke left in a heap in the gutter seemingly unconscious.

Claims from people at the time said: “Everyone should boycott the Red Lion until they get their house in order.”

A second wrote: "Disgusting bullies."

Similar complaints have continued to flood in – a post from four years later in September 2022 said: “DO NOT GO TO THIS PUB vile security walking around with knuckle dusters waiting to beat people up then it’s not just one of the bouncers it’s about five of them jump in they sell drugs in here and also sell balloons in here.”

Another, titled “Appalling behaviour from door staff” warned female guests against going.

It made a major unverified accusation saying: “Appalling behaviour from male security staff spat in my face, I’m a woman!

“Disgusting .... keep away, they have no morals, therefore I do wonder how they treat their wives. Ladies keep away.”

Allegations of violence are easy to find. As recently as March 27 one Facebook user posted an image of a man circled standing outside the door of the Red Lion.

The image was captioned: “The Red Lion, Benidorm absolutely shocking, bouncers bullying three on one! Bouncer in grey smacking a lad for enjoying himself because bouncer thinks he has higher power over everyone! N** head!”

Not everyone dislikes the famous bar, of course.

As well as the 169 reviews that are labelled "terrible" 136 are dubbed "excellent".

A review from January of this year said: "Great service and good pub food at great prices. Young lady who serves you is right on the ball.

"Lovely atmosphere and a great welcome".

Another five-star review said: "Prepare to sweat and dance an amazing place if you[r'e] looking for carnage don't take your Mrs only go on a stag do".

The Daily Star has contacted the Red Lion over the allegations of violence and selling of drugs.
