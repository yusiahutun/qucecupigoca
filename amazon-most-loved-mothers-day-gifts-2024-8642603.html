For the mom who has everything, you can’t go wrong with a classic Coach leather wristlet. Swarovski Stone Crystal Hoop Earrings

To wow Mom with a gift that looks way more expensive than it is, opt for these stunning Swarovski mini crystal hoop earrings for less than $50. Goop Beauty Dry Brush and Body Oil Bundle

Another top-tier beauty gift, the Goop Beauty Dry Brush and Body Oil Bundle will bring the spa home. It includes a dry brush, which gets rid of dead skin cells and smoothes out uneven texture, as well as body oil that’s full of hydrating and firming ingredients. Who wouldn’t want the gift of baby-soft skin for Mother’s Day?
