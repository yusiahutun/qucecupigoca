She welcomed daughter Westlyn, with actor husband Josh Brolin, in November.

And Kathryn Brolin shared an intimate picture of her topless and breastfeeding their three-month-old daughter, on Saturday. 

The 31-year-old personal assistant-turned-third wife revealed she had changed her attitude to seeing people breastfeeding, since becoming a mother herself. 

The mother-of-one is seen wearing only a pair of ripped jeans and a cowboy hat, in the black and white image. 

Sweet Westlyn is latched on and feeding from her momma.  

Kathryn - who married Goonies star Josh, 51, in 2016, explained that she may have previously misunderstood breastfeeding but now feels enlightened.

'As a fairly modest person, I couldn’t understand having something so intimate welcoming the eyes of others. I couldn’t imagine having someone other than my husband watch me messily feed our newborn. 

'I couldn’t imagine speaking openly with other women about what happens to your body after having a baby, or the carnage that ensues when a human takes their meals off a body part. 

She continued: 'From the moment this little woman arrived, and crawled without assistance to her new food source, I have been in complete and utter awe of the process of life-giving and life sustaining. Women are miraculous. 

'There is so much power held in this act. I feel so privileged to be able to experience motherhood and to have had any feelings of misunderstanding or non-understanding changed by this phase of my life that God has blessed me with.
