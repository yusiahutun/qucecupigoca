Selena Gomez Wears a Bow-Lined LBD to Steve Martin's Film Premiere

June 07, 2024
blog

Spring might have officially sprung, bringing with it a joyful pastel palette—but that doesn’t mean we must forgo black altogether. Selena Gomez certainly hasn’t. In fact, on Friday she made the inky hue look like the cheeriest color while stepping out in New York City.

Gotham//Getty Images

The “Love On” singer’s recent trip to France appears to be having a lasting effect, as Gomez served up another elegant Parisian-chic ensemble—and once again championed the coquette trend. For the outing, she looked timeless in a black midi dress that featured a pencil skirt, long sleeves, and an off-the-shoulder construction lined with huge satin bows all around. She kept things monochromatic with sheer tights; glossy, strappy, pointed-toe pumps; and a slim velvet clutch bag with yellow-gold hardware. Gomez eschewed a necklace, allowing her fanciful neckline to shine, but added plenty of diamonds via stacks of glittering rings and giant stud earrings by Pasquale Bruni. For glam, she opted for classic Parisian makeup, complete with a soft brown smoky eye, fluttery eyelashes, rosy cheeks, and a muted-red lip, and swept her chestnut locks into a sophisticated side-parted, slicked-back bun.

The Rare Beauty founder wore the elegant ensemble to support her Only Murders in the Building costar Steve Martin at the premiere of his new documentary, Steve! (Martin): A Documentary in 2 Pieces. Gomez showed her love for Martin by also taking to her Instagram Story to post a promo slide for the movie, which she captioned: “It pains me to know how many people may not know truly about Steve’s life. He’s truly an icon and tried and never gave up…my dear friend you are extraordinary @stevemartinreally.”

The Spring Breakers actor channeled the City of Love several times last month. On one occasion, she looked romantic in a black lace long-sleeved mock-neck top, layered over a matching camisole, all tucked into a flouncy pleated bubble skirt, which she wore over sheer black tights, a wide leather belt, and pointed-toe stiletto boots by Stuart Weitzman. While walking the streets of the city, she cut another gorgeous figure in a belted black minidress paired with matching tights, a leather coat, and shiny kitten heels adorned with bows.
