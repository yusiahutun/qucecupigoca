Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift’s first conversation after he and the Kansas City Chiefs won the Super Bowl has been revealed, and it’s just adorable.

Taylor Swift had quite the journey to make it to Vegas, as she jumped on a jet after finishing her back-to-back shows in Tokyo. Thankfully, due to the time difference, she was able to make it back to the States on time to see Travis Kelce in action, but her efforts didn’t go unnoticed as he adorably thanked her in their first conversation after the win.

Travis Kelce thanks Taylor Swift in ‘electric moment’

It’s the audio everyone has been waiting for, the first conversation between everyone’s favorite celebrity couple after the Super Bowl.

The convo has been shared by the NFL on X, formerly known as Twitter, and we honestly have tears in our eyes watching.

“Thank you for the support, thank you for coming, thank you for making it halfway across the world,” Travis emotionally says while they wrap their arms around each other.

“You’re the best baby, the absolute best,” he exclaims before asking her: “Was it electric?”
