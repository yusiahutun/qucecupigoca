Karlie Kloss says designers stopped working with her when she went from a size zero to a size four

March 10, 2024
blog

Karlie Kloss is one of the most famous models in the world, but she revealed her career almost came crashing down after she went on birth control and gained weight.

The 26-year-old became a runway star when she was just 15 years old, but by the time she was a household name, her changing body started costing her modeling jobs. 

'I started taking birth control, and my body became more womanly—hips and thighs appeared,' Karlie told Vogue, explaining that she went from a size 0 to a size 4/6.

'I started losing jobs; I wasn’t getting booked for the runway; designers stopped working with me,' she recalled. 'It felt as if my world had been turned upside down.'

Karlie admitted that she used to measure herself against Gisele Bündchen, but she realized the constant comparison to the famous model wasn't productive. 

'That’s when I had a breakthrough: I realized it was time for me to do me, to embrace the things that make me who I am,' she said. 

On her road to self-discovery, Karlie took a coding course at the Flatiron School in New York and enrolled in classes at New York University.  

The model, who took a hiatus from Victoria's Secret to pursue her education, went on to create Kode with Klossy, a scholarship program for teenage girls interested in tech.

In four years, her two-week summer camp for girls ages 13 to 18 has gone from a classroom of 20 in New York to a nationwide program that offers scholarships to 1,000 teenage girls across 25 cities.   

And while she once struggled to get jobs, Karlie is more famous than ever. In her latest campaign for Carolina Herrera's fragrance Good Girl, she channels her best Jessica Rabbit in a sultry red dress. 

It has been an exciting year for Karlie, who recently got engaged to her longtime beau Josh Kushner, 33, in June. 

'The proposal was romantic and sweet. We spent the weekend in upstate New York, just the two of us,' she told Vogue. 

Karlie waited about a month before announcing the news on social media in July, and her Instagram post earned a heartfelt comment from Joshua's sister-in-law, first daughter Ivanka Trump.

'So, so happy for you and Josh! I feel blessed to have you as a sister (!!!) Karlie and look forward to the decades of happy memories we will create together as a family! [sic]' the White House adviser wrote.   

Ivanka, 36, converted to Orthodox Judaism before she married Jared Kushner, 39, in 2009, and following her engagement to Josh, rumors swirled that Karlie had done the same.

Many have also questioned how close Karlie is to Ivanka, whose father's presidency has been mired in controversy.

The Victoria's Secret star neither confirmed nor deny the speculation that she has converted, noting that she finds the focus on her relationship to be sexist.

'At the end of the day, I’ve had to make decisions based on my own moral compass—forget what the public says, forget social media,' she said.

'I’ve chosen to be with the man I love despite the complications,' she added. 'It’s frustrating, to be honest, that the spotlight is always shifted away from my career toward my relationship. I don’t think the same happens in conversations with men.' 

Karlie did, however, point out that she and her fiancé Josh 'share a lot of the same liberal values.'

During the 2016 presidential, a spokesman for Josh told Esquire that the lifelong Democrat would not be voting for Donald Trump, despite his brother's connection to the campaign.

Karlie, meanwhile, used her social media platform and the hashtag '#ImWithHer' to draw support for Hillary Clinton. 

Josh attended the Women's March as 'an observer' in January 2017, and he also participated in the March for Our Lives gun-control demonstration in Washington, D.C. earlier this year.  

The two have been together since they were seated next to each other at a friend's dinner party when Karlie was just 19 years old.

'We’ve really grown together personally and professionally,' she said. 'Josh knows that I’m just a nerdy, curious human being. I think that’s why he loves me. We have each other’s back.'
