Top 10 best Quran reciters in the world: best voices today

May 26, 2024
blog

Since time immemorial, the holy Quran has been read and memorized in the Islamic religion. It has been argued that knowing how to read a sacred book and mastering it are entirely different things. On that note, there have emerged the best Quran reciters in the world whose mastering abilities are the envy of many within the religion.

The people who have adopted the art of recitation with perfection in a melodious voice are called Qur’anic reciters or simply Qaris. The Quran is a beautiful piece of literature written in the Arabic language. Unlike the Christian Bible, its chapter is referred to as surah and the verse is called ayah.

Best Quran reciters in the world

Here are some of the ten best Quran reciters you will find.

1. Abdul Rahman Ibn Abdul Aziz Al Sudais

Abdul Rahman Al Sudais was born on February 10th, 1960 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. He is of the Anza clan of the Najd area. During his childhood, Abdul was an excellent student. He had mastered the Holy Quran by the age of 12 years.

Currently, Rahman is the General President of the Affairs of the two Holy Mosques, commander in chief of the special forces of the security of the two Holy Mosques and the leading Imam at Masjid Al-Haram.

He studied at Al Muthana Bin Harith elementary school and graduated in 1979. He proceeded to study for a Bachelor’s degree in Shariah at Riyadh University. After his graduation in 1983, he enrolled for a master’s degree at Mohammad Bin Saud Islamic University and finished in 1995. He studied for his PhD at Umm Al Qura University.

Rahman is considered one of the best readers in the world. He has received many awards and recognitions. In 2005, for example, he received the "Islamic Personality of the Year.’"

2. Al-Afasy, Sheikh Mishary bin Rashid

Mishary Bin Rashid was born on September 5th, 1976, in Kuwait. He is among the most famous Quran reciters in the world. He is the Imam of Masjid Al-Kabir located in the city of Kuwait and is known for his exemplary recitation ability.

On October 25th, 2008, the Imam was awarded the first Arab Creativity Oscar by the Arab Creativity Union in Egypt.

The Sheikh has two Space channels, Alafasy TV and Alafasy Q. Sheikh Mishary.

In August 2009, he released a nasheed video called Ramadan under the Zain label where he sings in Arabic, English, and French. Muslims positively received these recordings, and as a result, most television stations broadcasted them throughout the holy month.

3. Al-Ajmi, Sheikh Ahmad Ali

Sheikh Ahmad Ali was born on February 24th, 1968, in Khobar Province, Saudi Arabia. Before he became famous for his recitation prowess, he studied the interpretation of the Quran in Pakistan and Saudi Arabia. He is an Imam at mosques in Jeddah and Khobar. For years he has perfected the art of reciting the Quran and as a result, he ranks among the best you will ever find.

4. Saad Al Ghamidi

Despite the fact that he studied Islamic law, Ghamidi has decided to devote much of his time and energy to Qur’anic recitation and nasheed. He memorized the Quran at the age of 22 years.

Saad’s soulful voice and Tajweed have attracted the attention of many people and thus a choice voice in several Radio and Television stations.

5. Saud Ibn Ibrahim Ibn Muhammad Al-Shuraim

Saud is a Quran reciter of the Hanbali denomination. He is most identified with his unique melodious voice during his Quran recitation. Sheikh Shuraim is a professor of Islamic law and Islamic studies. He boasts of vast knowledge of Islamic poetry, Aqeeda, and Figh.

He is one of the Imams and Khateeb at the Grand Mosque of Mecca, thanks to his academic achievements and beautiful voice that many Imams try to emulate.

He was awarded Islamic Personality of the Year in 2005 at the Dubai International Holy Quran awards.

6. Ulfah, Hajjah Maria

Hajjah Maria Ulfah was born on December 21st, 1955. She was born in Lamongan Regency, East Java, Indonesia and was introduced to the Quran recitation by her father. She began reciting the Holy book at the tender age of 6 and ever since she has never looked back. As a reciter, Maria has won two Indonesian Quran recitation contests. She has also won an international Quran recitation award in Malaysia emerging as the first female to win the prize.

She is married to Mukhtar Ikhsan, and their family is blessed with three children.

7. Al-Shatri, Sheikh Abu Bakr

Abu Bakr is a Saudi Imam and one of the Quran's readers. He was born in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia in 1970. He was raised in Jeddah. He studied 'Holy Quran Studies' and graduated from Cheikh Aymane Rochdi Suwaid in 1416 Hijri.

He is married and has four children.

8. Al-Mueaqly, Sheikh Maher bin Hamad

Al-Mueaqly was born on January 7th, 1969 in Madina. He is the Imam of the Grand Mosque in the Holy city of Mecca. He got a Masters in Shariaa from Umm Al Qura University in 2004. In the same year, he returned to Makkah and began to preach and recite the Holy Book.

He was appointed Imam in Masjidul Haram, Makkah in 2007 and ever since he has been leading Taraweeh. His beautiful Quran recitations have been featured on a TV satellite station called Al Arab.

9. Tablawi, Sheikh Mohammad

Mohammad Tablawi is recognized worldly as one of the famous Quran reciters in the world. He is an Egyptian Imam and Quran reader. He was born on November 14th, 1934.

He earned a degree in Al-Azhar’s Recitations in 1954.

10. Saud bi Ibrahim bin Muhammad Al-Shuraim

He is one of the Imams in the Grand Mosque (Masjid al-Haram) in Mecca. He was born on January 19th, 1964. Saud commenced his elementary schooling at Areen School and proceeded to middle school at Modern school. He then attended high school at Al Yarmouk North.

As a renowned Qari, he holds a PhD and is a professor of Sharia at the Umma al-Qura University in Makkah.
