What to Look For With a Phone Case in 2024

Clocking in at over $1,000, purchasing a reliable case is a necessity for your Galaxy S24 Ultra. Finding the right one for your needs isn't easy, as there are hundreds of options to choose from—and not all of them are up to the task. It's hard to go wrong with trusted brands like OtterBox, Spigen, and Smartish, though every so often a smaller brand will surprise with an affordable, durable, case.

Aside from sticking to trusted brands, the first thing you'll need to decide when shopping for a case is how much protection you need. Today's cases come in a variety of sizes. If you're prone to dropping your Galaxy S24 Ultra, consider investing in something rugged with plenty of grip. If you want something to protect your phone from scratches, you can get away with something much smaller. Since the S24 Ultra is already a large phone, you'll probably want to find something fairly thin, otherwise, it'll become unwieldy in your hand.

Customization options are also worth considering when looking for the best Galaxy S24 Ultra cases. If you don't mind a simple black or clear case, you'll have plenty of options. But if you want something flashier or with unique patterns, your options become much more limited.

Money is always a factor, and if you've already dropped over $1,000 on the Galaxy S24 Ultra, you may not be keen to drop $75 on a case. Thankfully, reliable cases can be found with a wide range of price tags. Rugged cases tend to be more expensive than their thin counterparts, though there are a few notable exceptions to this rule.

Here's a look at the best Galaxy S24 Ultra cases to help you with your search. Whether you need something rugged and durable or thin and stylish, there's bound to be something that catches your eye.

The below cases will only work with the Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra! They won't work with the standard Galaxy S24 or Galaxy S24+ models, so make sure you have the right case for your S24 model before you buy.

FAQ

What are the differences between the S24 models?

There are big differences between all Galaxy S24 models, including screen size and battery. The S24 Ultra also includes an S Pen. Check out Samsung's official comparison for more details.

What are the improvements to the Galaxy S24 Ultra from the S23 Ultra?

The Galaxy S24 Ultra features a better processor (Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 vs Snapdragon 8 Gen 2), better cameras, and generative AI features that provide you with tons of cool new features (such as Circle to Search). For more info, check out the official Samsung comparison page.

How do I transfer my data to a new phone?

If you're purchasing your phone from a carrier like AT&T or Verizon, data transfer is likely taken care of as part of the upgrade process. If you're upgrading on your own, look at our guide on how to transfer apps to a new Android device.

Does the S24 Ultra have a headphone jack?

No, the Galaxy S24 Ultra does not have a headphone jack. This has been standard practice in the industry for years, and it's unlikely to make a return.
