Who are Harry Holland’s parents?

Harry Holland and his famous brother are the sons of Dominic and Nikki Holland, who have been married since 1994, according to an old picture of Nikki as a bride shared on Instagram.

Dad Dominic works as a comedian, author and broadcaster. As stated on his website, he has written nine books and was behind the BBC Radio 4 series The Small World of Dominic Holland. According to his IMDB profile, he has also worked as a writer on British TV series including Brand Spanking New Show (2000), Unleaded (2014) and The Rob Brydon Show (2010). Their mum Nikki, who studied textiles at university with Dominic, also took up a different career after graduating. Per her website, she is a “highly experienced photographer” who specialises in head shots for actors, social media and companies.

Where did the Holland family grow up?

After having Tom Holland, Dominic and Nikki had twins Sam and Harry in 1999, and their youngest son, Paddy, in 2004. According to People, they were all raised in Kingston upon Thames in London, UK.

“We had this amazing foundation where, no matter the time of the day, either mum or dad would be at the house,” Tom said about his upbringing on the On Purpose with Jay Shetty podcast in 2023. “We felt so solid as a family … I really admire how my dad was around.”

What does Harry Holland do for a living?

Like his older brother and father, Harry has gone into the world of showbiz. Aside from his personal film projects – more on those in a minute – the 25-year-old has accompanied Tom on set a handful of times too, appearing in both The Impossible and Cherry with him.

According to Tom’s interview on The Graham Norton Show, Harry was also supposed to star in Spider-Man: No Way Home, but his scene didn’t make the final cut.

“We cast Harry and he’s on set. I go up to the stunt coordinator and I’m like, ‘Please, just whatever you do with the stunt, make it so he’s upside down.’ Bless him, he’s doing a really good job …” he said, adding, “But the icing on the cake – and I don’t think he actually knows this – we saw the film the other day and they’ve cut the scene. It’s not in the film.”

However, Harry’s real passion appears to be directing, and he has been behind six short films to date, including Roses for Lily and the aforementioned Last Call.

What films and TV shows has Harry Holland been in?

When it comes to acting, he’s not just following Tom Holland around Hollywood. Harry has been in nine films and TV shows in total, including Diana (2013) with Naomi Watts, Last Christmas in 2019, and played the part of the Mon Calamari Nobleman in an episode of The Mandalorian in 2023.

He is popular on social media

Harry has garnered an impressive number of fans in recent years. He now boasts 1.2 million followers on Instagram, where he mostly shares photos of himself golfing and hanging out with friends and family.

He’s close with his brother

It seems like the Holland brothers are all very close with one another. They regularly share photos together on Instagram.

In one post from 2021, Tom shared a shot with his brother on his shoulders, with the caption: “Today was easily one of the highlights of my career. For those of you that were there, you know what I’m talking about and for those of you that weren’t, you better buckle up!!!”
