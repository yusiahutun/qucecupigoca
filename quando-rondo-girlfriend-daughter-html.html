Quando Rondos Ex Accuses Him of Chasing Women Rather than Spending Time with Daughter

July 26, 2024
blog

Jai Doll has made some bold accusations against her ex-partner and rapper, Quando Rondo.

The social media personality — who shares daughter Italy Naomi with the rapper — recently took to her Twitter to share a series of tweets, where she accused Rondo of not being a responsible co-parent.

Jai Doll's Allegations against Quando Rondo

In one of her tweets, Doll claimed that the Georgia-born rapper often chased women and spent time with them rather than investing the same time with their nearly 2-year-old daughter. She also wrote in the same tweet that he had lately been claiming he wasn't the father of her baby.

"[Rondo] spend more time chasing [expletive] than with his daughter (that he claim ain't his all of sudden), it just don't make sense to me. But it's been that tho," she wrote in the tweet.

In another tweet, she stated that people thought she was mad, but according to her, most of the things between her and Rondo happened "behind the scenes," and no one knew what the rapper was up to.

Due to that, she said many had a false image of her while adding she could reveal the whole truth about her ex-boyfriend with one picture.

"Ask him why I LEFT HIM ? Ion get left," she wrote in another tweet.

Likewise, Doll stated that she was also ready to sign the papers, implying she was prepared to get into legal trouble if she needed to do for the sake of her and her daughter's betterment.

But despite all the allegations of being an irresponsible parent and ex-partner, Rondo has made no comments on the matter so far.

Quando Rondo Recently Posted Pictures with His New Girlfriend

Doll's bold statements against her ex-partner came a few hours after Rondo posted a series of pictures with his alleged new girlfriend, who goes by the username @kii.yaaah on Instagram.

In the snaps, the rapper and his new girlfriend were captured having some intimate moments. They were even seen kissing each other in the third picture of the slide.

Many of his followers immediately added that the girl looked young, possibly a high-school teenager. A few comments also revealed that the rapper had previously introduced the girl to his followers but as his sister.

"I THOUGHT U SAID THATS YO SISTER," someone commented in his post.

Jai Doll and Quando Rondo Welcomed Their Daughter in December 2019

Doll and Rondo became parents when they welcomed their baby girl, Naomi, on December 8, 2019.

The young mother, who has over 204,000 followers on Instagram, has also documented her pregnancy journey on her Instagram highlights section. In the highlight titled 'Italy Naomi,' she has added pictures from the time she found out about her pregnancy.

The highlight further features pictures and videos of her and Rondo celebrating Doll's pregnancy, baby shower, and the birth of Naomi.

At present, the little one has her own Instagram handle, too, presumably managed by her mother, with over 109,000 followers. But the handle only has 18 posts in the last two years.
