It has been said that beauty is in the eyes of the beholder. Today, several hairstyles have been invented to accentuate women's features or help them embrace those that might affect their confidence. Hairstyles for long faces and big foreheads are an excellent way of building your esteem and helping you accept your perceived flaws.

A woman's hair and face are a significant part of her. While some love their natural hair, others love trying different styles and haircuts. On the other hand, some ladies love their natural skin, while others prefer to wear makeup. Either way, remember that you are beautiful and complete, whatever your perceived flaws. So, what are the best hairstyles for long faces and big foreheads?

25 hairstyles for long faces and big foreheads

Women can play around with their hair colour and texture to achieve different looks. You might be wondering, ''What hairstyle suits a long face and a big forehead?'' Check out these ideas suggested by The Right Hairstyles and H Adviser that you can try out depending on your taste and preference:

1. Bob with full fringe

Bobs are a great way to even out your face. They are also stylish and allow for versatility and creativity. In addition, the fringe covers the forehead completely, making the face look smaller by removing attention from it.

2. Full bangs

If you want to kill two birds with one stone, hide your forehead and long face, then bangs are for you. Bangs provide a limitless variety of styles for any hair length. In addition, they add volume to your hair and help cover some face blemishes.

3. Top knot braided side bang

Braids are an excellent way to protect your hair, but they also give you room to create beautiful pieces of art. The top knot braided bang is one of the braiding hairstyles for big foreheads. This style draws attention to the top of your head instead of the forehead because the bang covers it partly.

4. Side knot bun

The side knot bun holds all your hair in a bun on the lower side of your head. At the front part, the hair is combed sideways, covering a significant portion of your forehead. This style is neat and has frequently been rocked by brides on their wedding day.

5. Long fringe

A long fringe brushes past the chin and can help to frame your long face and hide your forehead. This style is incredible for women with middle-length or long hair and is relatively easy to maintain. You can choose to part your hair at the side or off-centre before combing it down.

6. Curtain bangs

Curtain bangs frame your face and forehead the same way a curtain covers a window. They are shaggy and do not require much effort to achieve. Unlike regular bangs, curtain bangs are longer on the sides and make your forehead appear narrow.

7. Choppy layered wavy lob

The choppy layered wavy bob reduces your big forehead and flatters your facial features by adding hair volume on the sides. You can rock this hairstyle if you have short or middle-length hair. The waves add to your hair volume and are also easy to style.

8. Wispy bangs and layers

Wispy bangs look feathered at the ends. They are an excellent choice for women who want to make their long faces look shorter. Even better, you can add layers to this style to add to its beauty and elegance.

9. Voluminous curly bob

Voluminous bobs make your face appear narrow by covering your forehead in part. However, you can get dramatic and make the bob asymmetrical, leaving one side longer. The curls add to the beauty of this hairstyle as they also draw attention to your facial features.

10. Side-swept bangs

Bangs are a timeless way to cover your forehead. They are easy to attain and are low-maintenance. Side-swept bangs frame your face, highlighting your features and flattering your long face.

11. Curly bob and straight bangs

If you are a creative who loves to try out wild ideas on your hair, you can try out a curly bob with straight bangs. This is one of the hairstyles for women with big foreheads that brings out the girl in you while also adding style and elegance.

12. Long bob middle part

For ladies who do not mind their hair touching their faces, the long bob middle part is an excellent option and one of the natural hairstyles for big foreheads. This style cuts your forehead's length, making it appear smaller. This style also leaves your hair bouncy and can be a good option for ladies with mid-length hair.

13. Pin-up curly bangs

Pin-ups are girly and stylish, especially when you want to pull off a retro look. You hold your hair in a high ponytail and then use a curling iron to create a round faux bang covering your forehead's top half.

14. Medium pixie with bangs

This medium-sized haircut goes together with long side-swept bangs that are stylish and feminine. Moreover, they cover your forehead, and they can be styled sideways or at the front. This style also helps to flatter your long face.

15. Short pixie cut

You will often find a woman who has opted to go for a stylish haircut. The short pixie cut is an excellent short haircut for a big forehead and long face. The pixie trims the forehead at the right angles, making it appear smaller and narrower.

16. Voluminous side bang

Adding volume to your hair shifts attention so that it appears like the hair is big, not your forehead. Voluminous side bangs balance your face and make it look smaller.

17. Choppy bangs and top volume

You can play around with your ordinary bangs by chopping your hair in layers and adding waves to it. You can also add extra volume to the strands, leaving a wild but stylish look that distracts attention from your forehead.

18. Extra-long layers

This simple yet gorgeous hairstyle is ideal for long faces and big foreheads. The layers are combed right below your face, which helps balance it by adding volume to the hair. Additionally, it also evens out the forehead.

19. Side-swept bangs with a short bob

A short bob complements your long face and removes attention from your forehead. A side-swept hairstyle highlights your facial features and is an elegant option for your big forehead. You can also colour or highlight your strands to be more conspicuous.

20. Voluminous layers

You can add volume to your hair by layering it. Then, comb it down to achieve an elegant look that covers your forehead. Layers are an excellent option for ladies with thin strands. In addition, there are numerous ways to style your hair.

21. Simple off-centre part

This is one of the simple hairstyles for big foreheads that you can achieve without much effort. You part the hair off the centre of your head and comb it downwards. If you have long, middle-length, or short hair, you can try this look.

22. Long layered bob

Long bobs (Lobs) leave the hair below the chin and above the collar bone. You can add style to this trendy hairstyle by adding volume to your strands, which makes the lower half of your face look balanced compared to your forehead, and they also take away attention from it.

23. Asymmetrical razored bob

This elegant look makes your forehead appear smaller, especially when styled into a side part. The hair is cut into an uneven bob where one side is longer than the other, leaving some hair to cover the forehead.

24. Faux bang updo

Updos are fun and leave room for immense creativity. For example, you can hold your ponytail into a high bun and pull out a few ends for them to rest on your forehead. This style is neat and has a chic appearance that enhances your facial features.

25. Long parted bangs

You can comb the bangs to the side of your face, which will make your forehead appear smaller because a significant portion of it is cut off from people's view. This style is simple and chic and is also low-maintenance.
